FINAL DECISION AND ORDER

ISSUES

1. Is the Board of Education unilaterally changing the Student’s Individualized
Education Program?
2. If so, what is an appropriate remedy?

PROCEDURAL HISTORY

The Student submitted a Request for Impartial Special Education Hearing on August 9,
2012. The Hearing Ofﬁcer was assigned to the case on August 13, 2012. On August 17,
2012 and again on August 21, 2012 the Hearing Ofﬁcer attempted to conduct a
prehearing conference. A hearing date of October 2, 2012 was scheduled through e—mail
communication between the parties and the Hearing Ofﬁcer.

On September 27, 2012, the Student requested that the matter be dismissed with
prejudice. The hearing date of October 2, 2012 was cancelled.

FINAL DECISION AND ORDER

It is ordered that the Student’s request for dismissal is granted, and this case is dismissed
with prej udice.
