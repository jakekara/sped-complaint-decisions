FINAL DECISION AND ORDER

ISSUES:

1. Did the Board of Education provide a free appropriate public education for the
Student from April 21, 2015 forward?

2. If not, what are the characteristics of a free appropriate public education for the
Student?

3. If not, is the Student entitled to an out of district placement?

PROCEDURAL HISTORY:

The Student requested a special education due process hearing in the above-captioned
matter on May 5, 2015. The Impaltial Hearing Ofﬁcer on May 7, 2015. A telephonic pre-
hearing conference was held on June 1, 2015. Attorney Howard Klebanoff appeared on
behalf of the Student and Attorney Michelle Laubin appeared on behalf of the Board of
Education. It was established that the deadline for issuing the ﬁnal decision was July 17,.
2015. An evidentiary hearing was scheduled for June 30, 2015.

On June 11, 2015 the Student reported that the dispute had been resolved, and requested that
the case be dismissed.

FINAL DECISION AND ORDER:

It is ordered that the Student’s request for dismissal is granted and this matter is dismissed.
