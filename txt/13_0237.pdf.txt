FINAL DECISION AND ORDER

ISSUES:

1. Were the Individualized Education Program (IEP) and special education placement provided to
Student by the Board for the 201 1-2012 school year appropriate to his special education needs?

2. Were the IEP and special education placement provided to Student by the Board for the 2012-
2013 school year appropriate to his special education needs?

3. If either or both IEPs and placements were not appropriate to the Student’s special education
needs, is placement at the Speech Academy appropriate?

4. Is the Board responsible for funding placement of the Student at the Speech Academy?

PRO CEDURAL HISTORY:

Thjs hearing was requested by Parents on November 28, 2012, and the decision date was Febmary 1 1,
2013. The Hearing Ofﬁcer was appointed on November 29, 2012. At the pre-hearing conference on
December 17, 2012, it was reported that a resolution meeting had not been held and the Parties were
considering mediation. The hearing was scheduled for F ebruary 1 and 15, 2013, and the Palties requested
an extension of the decision date to March 13, 2013, to accommodate the hearing dates.

Mediation was requested and was scheduled for February 1, 2013, The Parties requested that the
February 1 hearing session be postponed and that the decision date be extended to provide for mediation.
Mediation did not resolve the dispute. The Parties requested postponement of the February 15 hearing
session to accommodate a Flaming and Placement Team (PPT) meeting. The Hearing Ofﬁcer extended
the decision date to April 12, 2013, and the hearing was re-scheduled to March 13 and 26, 2013.

The Parents’ Attorney withdrew the request for hearing on March 1, 2013: the PPT meeting had been
postponed because of a snow storm, and additional concerns were being considered.

FINAL DECISION AND ORDER:

The Parents’ request for a hearing having been withdrawn, this matter is DISMISSED without prejudice,
