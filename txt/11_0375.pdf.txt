FINAL DECISION AND ORDER

ISSUE:
Whether the Board failed to take the steps to assure parental participation in the manifestation

PPT by failing to schedule the PPT at a mutually agreed upon time and place in violation of 34
CPR. §300.322(a) et seq‘ and Conn. Agencies Regs. §10-76d-12.

PROCEDURAL fHSTORY/DISCUSSION:
The Board received this expedited request for hearing on March 30 and a prehearing conference

convened on April 8. On April 12, the Parent’s attorney submitted a request to withdraw the
request for due process. Therefore, this matter is dismissed, without prejudice.

FINAL DECISION AND ORDER

The matter is DISMISSED, without prejudice.
