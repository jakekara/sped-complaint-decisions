FINAL DECISION AND ORDER

ISSUES:

1. Did the Board of Education offer the Student at free appropriate public education?
2. If not, should the Student be placed in a therapeutic residential placement?

PROCEDURAL HISTORY:

The Student requested a special education due process hearing in the above-captioned matter
on June 20, 2016. This Impartial Hearing Ofﬁcer was appointed to hear the case on June 21,
2016. A telephonic pre—hearing conference was convened on July 6, 2016. Attorney Andrew
Feinstein appeared on behalf of the Student and Attorney Marsha Moses appeared on behalf
of the Board of Education. It was determined that the deadline for ﬁling the ﬁnal decision and
order is September 2, 2016. Evidentiary hearings were scheduled for August 2, 2016 and
August 5, 2016.

On July 27, 2016, the Student requested that the matter be dismissed without prejudice due to
a change in circumstances. The Board of Education consented to the Student’s request.
F INAL DECISION AND ORDER:

It is ordered that the Student’s request for dismissal is granted and this matter is dismissed
without prejudice.
