PROCEDURAL HISTORY/SUMMARY:

The Parent ﬁled the Due Process Complaint and Request for Hearing on June 26, 2014. The Hearing
Ofﬁcer was appointed on June 30, 2014 and conducted a Prehearing Conference on July 18, 2014. The
hearing was scheduled for September 9, 2014. On August 28, 2014, the Parent’s attorney requested a
postponement of the hearing date and extension of the mailing date of the Final Decision to continue
negotiations of a tentative settlement between the parties. The Parent’s request was granted and the
hearing was postponed to October 1, 2014 and the mailing date of the Final Decision was extended from
September 10, 2014 to October 10, 2014.

The hearing convened on October 1, 2014. The Hearing Request/Due Process Complaint was entered
into the record as H.O. Exhibit 1 and the parties’ Stipulation of F acts was entered as H.O. Exhibit 2.
Parents’ exhibits P—l to P-6 and Board exhibits B-1 t0 B—18 were entered into the record as full exhibits.
The Hearing Ofﬁcer granted the parties’ joint request for additional hearing dates and extension of the
mailing date of the F inal Decision to continue the hearing. Additional hearing dates of October 20 and
October 29, 2014 were scheduled and the mailing date of the F inal Decision was extended to November
7, 2014. The Parents presented two witnesses on the ﬁrst day of the hearing. They were Mother and
Kim L. Fitzgerald RN-B.C.FNP.
