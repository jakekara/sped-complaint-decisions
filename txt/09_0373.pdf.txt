 PROCEDURAL HISTORY/SUMMARY: 
  The surrogate parent filed a request for due process after the parties failed 
to agree on the needs of the Student at a PPT meeting. The 
letter requesting the Due Process hearing is dated March 5, 2009.  The matter was assigned to the hearing officer on Ma
rch 23, 2009. At a prehearing conference on April 10, 2009, the parties requested a thirty day continuance to pursue Mediation. The parties agreed to hold the first day of hearing on May 29, 2009. The parties scheduled mediation on May 21, 2009.  The parties were 
able to reach an agreement in mediation. The parties 
request the matter be dismissed with prejudice.  The 
matter was resolved within forty-five days, as 
extended, to allow time to mediate the matter.   
     FINAL DECISION AND ORDER:
  The matter is dismissed with prejudice.
