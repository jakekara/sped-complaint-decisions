 PROCEDURAL HISTORY  
 This hearing was requested by letter dated January 16, 2007 to the Board of Education.  
The State Department of Education ("SDE"
) received the request on January 19 and 
assigned this Hearing Officer 
to the case on January 22, 2007.  The Board received the 
request on January 19.  A prehearing conference was held on February 7, 2007.  The decision deadline was set at April 4, 2007.  The attorneys believed that the case was 
resolved on February 2 at the resolution meeti
ng.  The Parents' attorney indicated that he 
would withdraw the case if it were resolv
ed.  One hearing date was agreed on for 
February 28.  On February 16, the Parents' attorney sent a 
letter to the Hearing Officer stating that he wished to withdraw the hearing without prejudice pending receipt of 
certain documentation from the Board's attorney.  The hearing date was canceled.  On 
March 2, the Parents' attorney wrote to the 
Hearing Officer and advised that the question 
pertaining to the settlement had been resolved
 and that the Parents wished to withdraw 
this matter with prejudice.  
 FINAL DECISION AND ORDER 
 It is ordered that this case sh
all be dismissed with prejudice.
