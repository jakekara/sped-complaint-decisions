FINAL DECISION AND ORDER

ISSUES:

1. Is the Board of Education’s proposal for the Student for the 2015-16 School Year
appropriate?

2. If not, can an appropriate program be provided for the 2015—16 School Year at the
Board of Education’s Dwight Elementary School?

PROCEDURAL HISTORY:

The Student requested a special education due process hearing in the above-captioned
matter on April 21, 2015. This Impartial Hearing Ofﬁcer was assigned to the case on April
27, 2015. A telephonic pre—hearing conference was held on May 12, 2015. The Student’s
parents appeared on behalf of the Student and Attorney Michelle Laubin appeared on behalf
of the Board of Education. The deadline for ﬁling the ﬁnal decision was established to he
July 2, 2015 and an evidentiaiy hearing was scheduled for June 24, 2015.

On June 11, 2015 the Student reported that the dispute had been resolved, and requested that
the case be dismissed.
FINAL DECISION AND ORDER:

It is ordered that the Student’s request for dismissal is granted and this matter is dismissed.
