FINAL DECISION AND ORDER

ISSUE:

Whether the hearing ofﬁcer shall order that the Student be placed in a therapeutic diagnostic
setting as an appropriate interim alternative educational setting for not more than 45 school days,

pursuant to 34 CPR. §300.532.
PROCEDURAL HISTORY/DISCUSSION:

The request for an expedited hearing was received by the Parent on April 16, 2012 and a
prehearing conference convened on April 20, 2012. At the prehearing conference, the Board
reported that a resolution session convened on April 19, 2012.

On April 27, 2012 prior to the ﬁrst hearing date, the Board’s attorney submitted notiﬁcation that
the case was settled. Therefore, this matter is dismissed.
FINAL DECISION AND ORDER:

The matter is DISMISSED,
