SUMMARY AND PROCEDURAL HISTORY:

The Student has been identiﬁed with Autism and is entitled to receive FAPE as deﬁned in the
Individuals With Disabilities Education Improvement Act (IDEA) 20 U.S.C. §1401 et seq. and
Connecticut General Statute §10-76a et seq.

At a planning and placement team (PPT) meeting, the Parents rejected the program offered by
the Board for the 2012—2013 school year. The Parents requested placement at the Foundation
School for the 2012—2013 school year. The Board refused the Parents’ request.

On March 27, 2013, the Board received notice of the Parents’ request for due process. The
parties agreed to go to mediation in place of a resolution meeting. The mediation session was
scheduled.
