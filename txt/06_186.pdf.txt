 PROCEDURAL HISTORY  
 The Parents filed a due process hearing request with the Regional Scho
ol District and the State Department of Education (SDE) on 
June 23, 2006.  This Hearing Officer was assigned to the case on June 28.  A prehearing

August 7 mediation.  The hearing was canceled for August 21. 
 FINAL DECISION AND ORDER
