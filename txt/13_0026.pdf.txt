FINAL DECISION AND ORDER

ISSUES:

1. Did the Board provide the Student 21 free, appropriate, public education (FAPE) in the
least restrictive environment (LRE) during the 2010-2011 school year?

2. Did the Board provide the Student a FAPE in the LRB during the 201 1—2012 school
year?

3‘ Is the Board proposing an educational placement that will provide the Student FAPE

in the LRE for the 2012-2013 school year?
4. If the Board has failed to propose an educational placement that will provide the
Student FAPE in the LRE for the 2012-2013 school year, is the Parent’s proposed

placement appropriate?

SUthARY:

A special education hearing in the above—captioned matter was requested by the Student’s
attorney and received by the Board on July 12, 2012. Attorney Spencer appeared on behalf of
the Student and Attorney Laubin appeared on behalf of the Board during a pre-hearing
conference held by telephone on July 30, 2012. On September 21, 2012 Attorney Spencer
withdrew her request for due process with prejudice.

FINAL DECISION AND ORDER:

The matter is DISMISSED with prejudice.
