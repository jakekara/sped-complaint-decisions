Hearing Officer   FINAL DECISION AND ORDER  SUMMARY:  This matter was assigned on November 24, 2004, and a prehearing conference 
was held on December 2.  On December 17, the attorney for the Parents submitted a 
request for an extension of the mailing date of the decision so that the parties could 
attempt to settle the matter.  That request was granted in accordance with Section 10-76h-

9(e) of the Connecticut Regulations which provides for a 30-day postponement for 
settlement discussions between the parties. 
 On January 19, the attorney for the Parents submitted a notice that the parents 
have determined that the proceeding against the Board is premature.   In accordance with 

Section 10-76h-9(e) of the Connecticut Regulations, if the parties are not prepared to go forward with the hearing, the hearing officer shall dismiss the hearing request without 
prejudice.  FINAL DECISION AND ORDER:  The matter is 
DISMISSED, without prejudice.
