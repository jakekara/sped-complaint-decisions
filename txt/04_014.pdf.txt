       FINAL DECISION AND ORDER
    The prehearing conference in this matter was conducted on January 27, 2004.  By 

agreement of the parties, the hearing was set for February 9, 2004.  At the start of the 
hearing the parties requested and were granted time to attempt to settle the matter.  They 
reported back that they had reached a tentative agreement on all issues except one and 
were hopeful that they could resolve that but it was dependent on information not 
available at the time of hearing.  By agreement of the parties a new hearing date was set 

for February 24, 2004.  At the hearing the parties reported that they had reached an agreement and placed the terms of the agreement on the record. 
   FINAL DECISION ANDORDER  The matter is 
DISMISSED.
