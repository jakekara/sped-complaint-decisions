ISSUES:

SUMMARY and PROCEDURAL HISTORY:

The Student has not been identiﬁed as entitled to receive FAPE as deﬁned in IDEA 20 U.S,C. §1401 et
seq. and Connecticut General Statute §10—76a et seq. At a planning and placement team (PPT) meeting,
the Parent disagreed with the PPT’S ﬁnding that the Student was not eligible to receive specia1 education
and related sewices as deﬁned in IDEA. The Parent requested that the Student be identiﬁed as eligible.
The Board refused the Parent’s request. On August 26, 2012, the Board received notice of the Parent’s
request for due process. The patties agreed to go a resolution meeting,

An impartial hearing ofﬁcer was appointed on August 28, 2012 and a pre-hearing conference was held
on September 6, 2012. A hearing date of October 10, 2012 was chosen by the parties. In an electronic
transmission dated October 9, 2012, the hearing ofﬁcer was advised that the Parent had retained counsel.

At the October 10, 2012 hearing date, a letter was read on the record that the Parent was withdrawing
the matter without prejudice. The withdrawal was granted, The date for mailing of the ﬁnal decision
and order is November 9, 2012.

FINAL DECISION AND ORDER:

THE MATTER IS DISMISSED WITHOUT PREJUDICE.
