FINAL DECISION AND ORDER
ISSUES:

1. Did the Board of Education provide the Student a free appropriate public education between
November 14, 2014 and June 9, 2016?
2. If not, is the Student entitled to compensatory education services?

PROCEDURAL HISTORY:

The Student requested a special education due process hearing in the above—captioned matter on
November 14, 2016. This Impartial Hearing Ofﬁcer was appointed to hear the case on
November 14, 2016. A telephonic pre-hearing conference was convened on December 6, 2016.
Attorney Elizabeth Adams appeared on behalf of the Student and Attorney Linda Yoder
appeared on behalf of the Board of Education.

The initial deadline for issuing the ﬁnal decision was January 27, 2017. A hearing was
scheduled for February 1, 2017. On December 6, 2016, the parties jointly submitted a written
request for a thirty—day postponement and extension of the timelines to conduct the hearing and
to ﬁle the ﬁnal decision in this case to February 24, 2017. The puipose of the requested
postponement and extension was to accommodate the hearing date and allow the parties to
participate in mediation. The request was granted. On J anuary 30, 2017, the Student requested
that the matter be withdrawn or dismissed.

FINAL DECISION AND ORDER:

It is ordered that the Student’s request is granted and the matter is dismissed.
