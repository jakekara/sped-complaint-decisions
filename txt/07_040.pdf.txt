 PROCEDURAL HISTORY

On March 26, 2007 the parents requested leave to obtain counsel and the LEA requested the parent consider a resolution they offered; the parties agreed to extend the date for final decision on the record to June 11, 2007.  On May 4, 2007 the parent through counsel requested the matter be dismissed without 
prejudice. The matter was resolved within 
all the statutory timelines as extended by 
requests of the parties.    FINAL DECISION AND ORDER
