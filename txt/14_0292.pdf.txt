PROCEDURAL HISTORY AND SUMMARY:

On November 25, 2013, the Bridgeport Board of Education and Achievement First Academy
received a request for special education due process hearing. The undersigned hearing ofﬁcer
was appointed on December 2, 2013. On December 17, 2013, a preheating conference was held
