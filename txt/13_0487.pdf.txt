FINAL DECISION AND ORDER
ISSUES:
1. Whether the Board’s proposed program for the Student at REACH is appropriate;
2. if not, whether the Student shall be placed at Trumbull High School as requested by the
Parent.

PROCEDURAL HISTORY/DISCUSSION:

This hearing request was received by the Board on May 22, 2013 and a preheating conference
convened on May 30, 2013.

On July 1, 2013, the Parent submitted notiﬁcation that the matter had been resolved through a

mediated agreement and that the Parent was withdrawing her due process hearing request, with
prejudice. Therefore, this case is dismissed.

FINAL DECISION AND ORDER:

The matter is DISMISSED.
