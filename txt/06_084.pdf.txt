 Appearing before: Attorney Patricia M. Strong, Hearing Officer   FINAL DECISION AND ORDER  PROCEDURAL HISTORY   The Parents filed a due process hearing request with the State Department of Education 
(SDE) on March 21, 2006.  This Hearing Officer was assigned to the case on March 22. The Board received the due process request on March 20, 2006.  A prehearing conference was held on April 18 at which time the partie
s agreed to schedule hearing dates on May 15 and June 1, 2006.  The decision deadline was extended from June 5 to June 25 to 
accommodate the June 1 hearing date.  On Ma
y 3 the Parents' attorney wrote to the 
Hearing Officer and stated that a settlement 
was reached at a resolution meeting and that 
the Parents wished to withdraw the due process hearing without prejudice until an agreement could be drafted and executed, at
 which time they would withdraw with 
prejudice.  As of June 13 no further correspondence was received from the parties.  The 
Hearing Officer cancelled both hearing dates.  FINAL DECISION AND ORDER  It is ordered that this case be dismissed with prejudice.
