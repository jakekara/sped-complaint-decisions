  FINAL DECISION AND ORDER    The prehearing conference in this matter was conducted on February 1, 2005 at which 
time hearing dates were scheduled by mutual agreement.  One of the issues was an out of 
district placement in a private school.  The date for mailing of the final decision was 
extended by agreement to April 29, 2005.  At the first day of hearing on March 2, 2005 
the parties requested time to pursue settlement negotiations and this was granted.  They 
reported back that they had reached agreement and agreed that the matter should be 

dismissed with prejudice.   
 FINAL DECISION AND ORDER  The matter is 
DISMISSED WITH PREJUDICE.
