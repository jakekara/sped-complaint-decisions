Strong Hearing Officer   FINAL DECISION AND ORDER   PROCEDURAL HISTORY   The Parents filed a due process hearing request on June 29, 2005.  This Hearing Officer was assigned to the due process case on June 30, 2005.  A prehearing conference was scheduled on July 8.  On July 6 Dr. Lombardo wrote to the Hearing Officer requesting a postponement of the prehearing conference because the Board's attorney was on vacation until July 11.  The Hearing Officer wrote to the parties advising them that the July 8 prehearing was canceled and asking them to choose a mutually convenient date from the alternate times listed in the prehearing notice.  On July 7 the Mother faxed a note to the Hearing Officer and Dr. Lombardo indicating that the Parents wished to withdraw the due process request.  FINAL DECISION AND ORDER  It is ordered that this case is dismissed without prejudice.
