Slez, Hearing Officer   NOTICE OF DISMISSAL   All parties appeared on May 23, 2002, the date previously scheduled for the first day of hearing.  At that time, counsel for the parties represented that settlement had been reached and requested additional time for the preparation of a final written agreement.  On June 24, 2002, counsel for the Board stated in writing that the settlement agreement had not yet been finalized and requested additional time to do so.  More than thirty (30) days have passed since the second request for additional time but there has been no further communication from either of the parties. FINAL DECISION AND ORDER: This matter is dismissed.
