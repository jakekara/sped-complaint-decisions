  FINAL DECISION AND ORDER  The prehearing in this matter was conducted on April 26, 2005.  The parties agreed to 
begin the hearing on May 13, 2005.  The original date for mailing of the final decision 
was May 31, 2005.  The Parent filed a Motion to Enforce Stay Put or for Homebound Instruction and a Motion for Order relative to placing the child in ACES to allow the parent to observe the program and the team at ACES.  The Board filed objections to these 
motions. Prior to the hearing the Parent reported that the matter had been resolved.  
 FINAL DECISION AND ORDER:  The hearing is dismissed.
