 PROCEDURAL HISTORY  
 This hearing was requested on July 18, 2007 by the Parents (Mother), who sent a form to the State Department of Education ("SDE"). 
 This Hearing Officer was assigned to the 
case on July 20.  The Board received a copy of the complaint on July 23.  On August 2, 
the Board's attorney filed an appearance.  
On August 3, a prehearing conference was held 
with the parties, who agreed to mediation. 
 A hearing date was scheduled for September 13, and the decision deadline was set at October 9, 2007.  On August 9, the Hearing Officer received a notice from the SDE that mediation 
was scheduled on August 20.  On August 22, the SDE sent a notice to the Hearing 
Officer stating that the parties reached agreement at the me
diation and that the hearing request was withdrawn.   FINAL DECISION AND ORDER 
 It is ordered that this 
case shall be dismissed.
