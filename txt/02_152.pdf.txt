Slez, Hearing Officer   NOTICE OF DISMISSAL   All parties appeared on July 15, 2002, the date previously scheduled for the first day of hearing.  At that time, counsel for the parties represented that the parties had reached a tentative agreement, the terms of which needed the approval of the Board superintendent.  On July 31, 2002, counsel for the parents notified the hearing officer that the terms of the settlement had been approved and requested two (2) additional weeks during which time counsel for the Board would be preparing the final written agreement. Although more than two (2) weeks have passed there has been no further communication from either of the parties.  FINAL DECISION AND ORDER: This matter is dismissed.
