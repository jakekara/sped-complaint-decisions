FINAL DECISION AND ORDER

ISSUE:

Whether the Board’s evaluations of the Student are appropriate

PROCEDURAL HISTORY/DISCUSSION:

The request for hearing was submitted by the Board on February 9, 2012 and a preheating
conference convened on February 14, 2012. At the prehearing conference, the Parent conﬁrmed
that she was not seeking payment for the Student’s follow up evaluation from the Board. At the
prehearing conference, the Board’s attorney conﬁrmed that the Board would consider any
evaluation completed by the Parent,

After the prehearing conference, the Board’s attorney requested that this matter be dismissed
because the outside evaluation will be paid by the Parent, and based on the understanding that

the obligation of the Board would be to consider the evaluation, Therefore, this matter is
dismissed.

F INAL DECISION AND ORDER:

The matter is DISMISSED.
