FINAL DECISION AND ORDER

ISSUES:

1. Whether the Student is eligible for special education;
2. Whether the Board denied the Student a free appropriate education by failing to evaluate

the Student;
3‘ Whether the Board shall reimburse the Parents for the cost of placement at Winston Prep

for the 2012-13 and 2013-14 school year;
4. Whether the Student is entitled to compensatory education,

PROCEDURAL HISTORY/DISCUSSION:

This hearing was requested on March 19, 2013 and assigned to Hearing Ofﬁcer Mary H.B.
Gelfman. The case was reassigned to the undersigned hearing ofﬁcer on June 12, 2013 after
Hearing Ofﬁcer Gelfman recused herself from this hearing.

The initial hearing date of June 19, 2013 was cancelled due to the unexpected family medical
issue for the Board’s attorney. The hearing convened on June 24, 2013. At the hearing on June
24, 2013 attorneys-for both parties were provided an opportunity to continue settlement
discussions. The parties agreed in principal to a settlement agreement and the case was
continued to July 1, 2013, Prior to the second hearing date, the Parents’ attorney submitted
notiﬁcation that the Parents withdraw their request for due process with prejudice. Therefore,

this case is dismissed.
FINAL DECISION AND ORDER:

The matter is DISMISSED.
