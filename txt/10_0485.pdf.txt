Appearing before: Attorney Elisabeth Borrino, Hearing Officer FINAL DECISION AND ORDER ISSUES: Whether the Board should pay for the Student&#8482;s placement at a post-12th grade program 
SUMMARY: On June 3, 2010, the Board received the Request for Due Process filed by the Parent.  Prehearing Conferences 
were held on June 29, 2010 and July 14, 
2010 and hearing dates were scheduled.  The Request was initially filed by the 
Parent. On July 1, 2010 the Student, being over the age of 
eighteen, filed his/her own Request which was consolidated with the Parent&#8482;s. On August 3, 2010, 
the Student and Parent withdrew the request for hearing without prejudice.  FINAL DECISION AND ORDER: The matter is 
DISMISSED without prejudice.
