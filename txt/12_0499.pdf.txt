FINAL DECISION AND ORDER

ISSUE:

Did the Board offer an appropriate program for the school year 2012—2013?

SUMMARY:

Parent initiated a Request for Due Process Hearing on June 13, 2012, which was received
by the Board on June 19, 2012. The original mailing date of the Final Decision was
September 12, 2012. The Board answered the Due Process Hearing Complaint and ﬁled a
Sufﬁciency Challenge on June 25, 2012. A Prehearing Conference was held on June 28,
2012 and a hearing scheduled for August 20, 2012. The Board’s Sufﬁciency Challenge was
granted and the Parent was ordered to ﬁle an Amended Complaint, On July 5, 2012, Parent
notiﬁed the Hearing Ofﬁcer that the parties had come to an agreement of the dispute at the
Resolution Meeting and she was withdrawing the Request of Due Process Hearing.

FINAL DECISION AND ORDER:

The matter is DISMISSED WITHOUT PREJUDICE.
