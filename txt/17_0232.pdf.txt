FINAL DECISION AND ORDER
ISSUES:

1. Did the Board of Education offer the Student a free appropriate public education for the
2015/2016 and 2016/2017 School Years?

2. If not, was The Windward School appropriate for the Student?

3. If The Windward School was appropriate, should the Student be placed there and/or
reimbursed for the expenses of the program?

4. Is the Student entitled to compensatory education services?

PROCEDURAL HISTORY:
The Student requested a specia1 education due process hearing in the above—eaptioned matter
on November 28, 2016. This Impartial Hearing Ofﬁcer was appointed to hear the case on

November 29, 2016. A telephonic pre-hearing conference was scheduled to take place on
December 21, 2016.

On December 20, 2016, the Student notiﬁed the Hearing Ofﬁcer that the case was settled at a
resolution session and requested that the matter be withdrawn or dismissed with prejudice.

FINAL DECISION AND ORDER:

It is ordered that the Student’s request is granted and the matter is dismissed with prejudice.
