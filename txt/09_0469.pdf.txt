 FINAL DECISION AND ORDER
 A special education hearing in the above-captione
d matter was requested by the Student via letter 
dated May 13, 2009. It was received by the Board of Education on May 15, 2009. The 30-day resolution period ran through June 14, 2009.  The deadline for ma
iling the final decision and order is July 29, 2009. 
A pre-hearing conference was held on May 29, 2009. Attorney Anne Eason appeared on behalf of the Student and Attorney Andrea Bellach appeared on behalf of the Board of Education. The following issues were identified: 
1. Whether the Board of Education failed to provide an appropriate program to Student 
during the portion of the 2006-07 school year wh
ich fell within the appropriate statute of limitations period; 
2. Whether the Board of Education failed to provide an appropriate extended school year program to Student during the summer of 2007; 
3. Whether the Board of Education failed to provide an appropriate program to Student 
during the 2007-08 school year; 4. Whether the Board of Education failed to provide an appropriate extended school year program to Student during the summer of 2008; and 
5. Whether the Board of Education failed to provide an appropriate program to Student 
during the 2008-09 school year. The parties participated in mediation and came to
 a settlement agreement. On June 24, Attorney 
Eason withdrew the Student&#8482;s request for hearing with prejudice. FINAL DECISION AND ORDER
 In light of the above facts, the above-captioned case is hereby dismissed with prejudice.
