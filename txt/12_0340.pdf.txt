FINAL DECISION AND ORDER
ISSUES:

1. Whether the Board’s failure to provide the Student the required AT in a timely manner
has resulted in a denial of FAPE;

2. Whether the Board shall provide the Student 21 computer installed with Kurzweill and
Dragon Speak, as well as a head set and microphone compatible with Dragon Naturally

Speaking;

3. Whether the Student is entitled to compensatory education.

PROCEDURAL HISTORY/DISCUSSION:

The request for hearing was received by the Board on March 19, 2012 and a preheating
conference convened on March 29, 2012. At the prehearing conference, the Board reported a
resolution session was scheduled to convene on April 2, 2012.

On April 13, 2012, prior to the ﬁrst hearing date, the Parent’s attorney submitted notiﬁcation that
the case was settled. Therefore, this matter is dismissed

FINAL DECISION AND ORDER:

The matter is DISMISSED,
