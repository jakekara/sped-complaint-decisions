[FAPE] 2007-08 and 2008-09 school years, in regular classes with supplementary aids and

Student for the 2009-10 school year, in regular classes with supplementary aids and services; and  
3. Whether the Student is entitled to compensatory education.   SUMMARY:  The Board received this request for hear
ing on May 27, 2009 and prehearing conferences

ation date in this matter.

the case had settled through a written mediation

  The matter is
