FINAL DECISION AND ORDER
ISSUES:

1. Was the program provided by the Board for the 2013-2014 school year appropriate and did it provide
the Student with a free and appropriate public education (FAPE) in the least restrictive enviromnent

(LRE)? Ifnot;

2. Is the unilateral placement of the Student, at the Kildonan School in Amenia, New York, appropriate
and does it provide the Student with an appropriate education?

3. Should the Board be responsible for the cost of the unilateral placement of the Student at the Kildonan
School for the 2013—2014 school year including transportation and related scwices?

41 Is the Student entitled to Compensatory Education for the denial of FAPE?

SUMMARY AND PROCEDURAL HISTORY:

The Student has been identiﬁed with Speciﬁc Learning Disability and is entitled to receive FAPE as deﬁned in
the Individuals With Disabilities Education Improvement Act (IDEA) 20 U.S.C. §1401 et seq. and Connecticut

General Statute §10—76a et seq.

At a planning and placement team (PPT) meeting, the Parents rejected the program offered by the Board for the
2013—2014 school year. The Parents requested placement at the Kildonan School in Amenia, New York, for the
2013-2014 school year. The Board refused the Parents’ request. On September 10, 2013, the Board received

notice of the Pal‘cnts’ request for due process. The parties agreed to forego a resolution meeting and mediate the

matter, The mediation date was pending.

An impaltial hearing ofﬁcer was appointed on September 10, 2013 and a pre—hearing conference was held on
September 20, 2013. A hearing date of November 8, 2013 was chosen by the panics. At the November 8, 2013
hearing date, the Parents’ attorney withdrew the matter without prejudice. There was no objection by the Board.
The mailing date for the F inal Decision and Order is November 22, 2013.

FINAL DECISION AND ORDER:

THE MATTER IS DISMISSED.
