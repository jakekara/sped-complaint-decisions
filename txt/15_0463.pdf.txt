SUMMARY AND PROCEDURAL HISTORY:

The Student has been identiﬁed as Speech or Language Impaired and is entitled to receive FAPE
as deﬁned in the Individuals With Disabilities Education Improvement Act (IDEA) 20 U.S.C.
§1401 et seq. and Connecticut General Statute §10-76a et seq.

At a planning and p1acement team (PPT) meeting, the Parents rejected the program offered by
the Board for the 2015-2016 school year. The Parents requested continued placement of the
Student at the Greenwich Education Group. The Board refused the Parents’ request. On April
15, 2015, the Board received notice of the Parents’ request for due process. The parties agreed to
a resqution meeting The resolution meeting date was May 6, 2015.
