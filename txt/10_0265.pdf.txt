FINAL DECISION AND ORDER 
ISSUES: 1. Whether the Board should reimburse the 
Parents for certain evaluations/observations performed by Dr. Ciocca; 2. Whether the Board should reimburse the Pa
rents for a Speech and Language evaluation; 3. Whether the Board failed to timely comp
lete an Occupational Therapy evaluation; 
4.Whether the Board committed procedural violations; 
5. Whether the Board should approve Dr. Cio
cca performing the neuropsychological evaluation; 
6.Whether the Board failed to provide FAPE; 
7.Whether the Board should provide compensatory education to the Student. 
SUMMARY: The Board received this request for hearing filed by the Parents on January 5, 2010. A Prehearing 
Conference was held on January 29, 2010 and Hearing dates were scheduled.  On March 3, 2010, pursuant to 
the Request of the Parents, the deadline for mailing the Final Decision and Order was extended to April 21, 
2010.  On April 1, 2010, Counsel for the Parents notifie
d the Hearing Officer that the parties had executed a settlement agreement and withdrew the Request for Due Process with prejudice.  
FINAL DECISION AND ORDER: 
The matter is DISMISSED, with prejudice.
