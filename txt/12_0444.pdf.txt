FINAL DECISION AND ORDER

ISSUES:

1. Whether the Board provided an appropriate individualized education program for the
school years 2010-2011 and 2011—2012?

2. Whether the Board should be required to reimburse the parents for the altemative
placements?

3. Whether the Board should required to reimburse the residential placement of Student
for the 2010-20] 1 school year?

4. Whether the Board should be required to ﬁnancially support the alternative placement
for the 2012-2013 school year?

5. Whether the Board violated Student’s procedural and substantive due process rights?

SUMMARY:

Parent initiated a Request for Due Process Hearing dated May 24, 2012, which was
received by the Board the same day. A prehearing conference was held on June 5, 2012. The
parties jointly requested an extension of the timelines in order to engage in mediation with a
state appointed mediator in good faith. The Hearing Ofﬁcer granted the Request and
scheduled a hearing date of August 21, 2012. The original mailing date of the Final
Decision was extended from August 7, 2012 to September 7, 2012.

Prior to the hearing on June 19, 2012, the parent’s attorney informed the Hearing Ofﬁcer
by email that mediation was scheduled for August 28, 2012, and that after a discussion with
the Board’s attorney, the parent withdrew the Request for Due Process Hearing without
prejudice to be re-ﬁled at a later time should mediation be unsuccessful.

FINAL DECISION AND ORDER:

The matter is DISMISSED without prejudice.
