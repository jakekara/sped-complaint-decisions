FINAL DECISION AND ORDER

A special education hearing in the above-captioned matter was requested by the Board of
Education (“BOB”) via letter dated June 25, 2012.] It was received by Student’s Attorney on
June 29. The deadline to mail the ﬁnal decision and order is August 19.

A telephonic pre-hearing conference was held on July 9. Attorney Meuser appeared on
behalf of BOB and Attorney Mascolo appeared on behalf of Student. The following issue is

identiﬁed: Should the Board of Education reimburse Student’s Parents for an independent

psycho-educational evaluation of Student?
Hearings were scheduled for July 31 and August 2.
Via letter dated July 24, 2012, BOE’S Attomey indicated that the parties entered into a

mediation agreement that resolves the legal issues in this case and withdrew the due process

request.

F INAL DECISION AND ORDER
In light of the above facts, the above—captioned case is hereby dismissed.
