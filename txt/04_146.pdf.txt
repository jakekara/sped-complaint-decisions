Hearing Officer   FINAL DECISION AND ORDER  ISSUE  The Board of Education request due process to pursue evaluation of the student.  PROCEDURAL HISTORY  The Board of Education requested due process on June 2, 2004.  At a prehearing conference the parties agreed to hearing dates.  A hearing convened on July 6, 2004, the court reporter was not in attendance due to an automobile accident.  The parties engaged 
in settlement discussions and set an additional date for hearing.  On August 13, 2004 the 
moving party believed the matter was resolved 
and requested dismissal without prejudice. 
 FINAL DECISION AND ORDER  The case is dismissed without prejudice.
