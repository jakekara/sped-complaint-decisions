   FINAL DECISION AND ORDER  ISSUES:  1. Whether the local educational agency provides the child with a free and appropriate 
public education.  2. Whether the child is entitled to compensatory education.  
 PROCEDURAL HISTORY  The parent requested a Due Process hearing on September 9, 2004.  There was a 
prehearing conference on September 16, 2004.  The parties were going to decide if they 
were available for hearing on October 6 and 7, 2004 or preferred to attempt settlement.  
There has been no further communication in this matter. 
 FINAL DECISION AND ORDER  The case is dismissed without prejudice.
