    FINAL DECISION AND ORDER   ISSUES:  1.  Whether the Student should be identified as eligible for special education and related 
services.  2.  If so, whether the Board shall provide the Student a free, appropriate, public education.  3.  Whether the Student is entitled to compensatory education. 
 4.  Whether the Board failed to evaluate the Student. 
 5.  Whether the Student is entitled to an independent psychological evaluation.

scheduling of hearing dates.  Thereafter, the mailing date was extended 21 days, and an
