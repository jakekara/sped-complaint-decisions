FINAL DECISION AND ORDER

ISSUES:

Shall the decision to deny funding of an independent Speech/Language evaluation of the Student
by a practitioner who does not ho1d a Connecticut Speech/Language certiﬁcate be sustained?

PROCEDURAL HISTORY:

This hearing was requested by the Board on October 22, 2012. The Hearing Ofﬁcer was
appointed on October 23, 2012. The date for mailing the decision was incorrectly given as
December 22, 2012: when the Board initiates a hearing, there is no conciliation period and the
decision date in this case is December 6, 2012.

At the pre—hearing conference on November 21, 2012, the Parties informed the Hearing Ofﬁcer
that mediation was scheduled for November 27, 2012. The hearing was scheduled for December

18, 2012.
STATEMENT OF JURISDICTION:

This matter was scheduled to be heard as a contested case pursuant to Connecticut General
Statutes (C.G.S.) §10-76h and related regulations, 20 United States Code §1415(f) and related
regulations, and in accordance with the Uniform Administrative Procedure Act (U.A.P.A.),

C.G.S. §§4-176e to 4-178, inclusive, §§4-181a and 4-186.

FINAL DECISION AND ORDER:

This matter, having been settled in mediation by the Paﬂies, and the request for hearing having
been withdrawn, is DISMISSED.
