 PROCEDURAL HISTORY  
 The Parents filed a due process hearing request with the State Department of Education 
(SDE) and the Ridgefield Board of Education on February 6, 2007.  This Hearing Officer was assigned to the case on February 7.  A prehearing conference was held on February 21.  At the prehearing conference the parties agreed to mediate and hearing dates were 
scheduled for March 29 and April 4 and 10.  
The decision deadline was set at April 23.  
On March 9 the Parents' attorney requested 
a postponement of the April 4 hearing date, 
which was granted.  The Hearing Officer also changed the Apr
il 10 hearing date to April 9 and extended the decision deadline to May 3, 2007.  On March 19, the Parents' attorney 
requested a postponement of the March 29 hearing date because the date was scheduled 
for mediation and of the April 9 hearing date 
because not all parties were available.  The 
request was granted and the hearing was rescheduled for May 2.  The decision deadline 
was extended to May 29, 2007.  On March 30 the Hearing Officer was notified that the parties had not reached an agreement at the March 29 mediation and to proceed with the 

hearing.  On April 25, the Board's attorney wrote to the Hearing Officer that the parties 
had reached a settlement and were in the proc
ess of drafting an agreement.  She requested 
that the May 2 hearing be canceled.  On April 26, the Hearing Officer advised the parties that the hearing was canceled and th
at the case would be dismissed.  
 FINAL DECISION AND ORDER 
 It is ordered that this case be dismissed.
