Hearing Officer   FINAL DECISION AND ORDER   PROCEDURAL HISTORY:  The student is a twelve year old child who has been diagnosed as autistic and has been determined eligible for special education and related services as defined in 20 U.S.C. &#167;&#167; 
1401 et esq. The parents requested a due process hearing on March 19, 2004 and a pre-hearing conference was conducted on April 1, 2004. At the pre-hearing conference hearing dates were set for April 30, May 14 and May 25, 2004.  The parents filed a Motion for An Independent Evaluation/ Observation of District&#8482;s Proposed Program. This Motion was timely objected to by the Board. The parents and the 
Board advised the Hearing Officer, on May 25, 2004, that an agreement had been reached 
by the parties and no further hearing dates were required. The parent submitted a letter 
stating that the matter was fully resolved.  
  FINAL DECISION AND ORDER  THE MATTER IS DISMISSED, WITH PREJUDICE.
