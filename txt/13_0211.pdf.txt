FINAL DECISION AND ORDER
ISSUES:

1. Did the Board of Education (BOE) offer the Student a free appropriate public education (FAPE) for the
2010/201 1 school year after November 13, 2010, and for the 2011/2012 and 2012/2013 school years?

2. If not, is Winston Preparatory an appropriate placement for the Student?
3. If so, is the Student entitled to reimbursement for the expenses of Winston Preparatory and/or for tutoring

and therapeutic sewices obtained by the Student at the Student’s own expense?
4. Is the Student’s program appropriate?
5. If not, what are the appropriate remedies?

PROCEDURAL HISTORY:

The Student requested a special education hearing in the above-captioned matter on November 13, 2012. This
Impartial Hearing Ofﬁcer was appointed to the case on November 19, 2012. A telephonic pre—hearing
conference was conducted on December 6, 2012. At that time, a hearing was scheduled for March 19, 2013.

Subsequently, the parties scheduled a mediation for March 19, 2013; the hearing on that day was rescheduled to
April 11, 2013. Unfoxtunately, the mediation was then rescheduled due to events beyond the paities’ control,
and planned for April 11, 2013. New hearing dates of May 23 and May 24, 2013 were scheduled. On April 25,
2013, the Student notiﬁed the Impartial Hearing Ofﬁcer that the Student and the Board of Education had
resolved their dispute, and requested that the matter be dismissed without prejudice

FINAL DECISION AND ORDER:

It is ordered that the Student’s request for dismissal is granted, and this case is dismissed without prejudice. ,
