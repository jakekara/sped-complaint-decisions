FINAL DECISION AND ORDER

Issues:

At the time of lhe pre-hearing conference, the Parents were in the process of reviewing a
proposed Individualized Education Program (“IEP”) that had been provided by the Board. The
panties agreed to hold off identiﬁcation of speciﬁc issues until after the proposed IEP was
reviewed by the Parents. The parties also agreed to request mediation in an attempt to rectify

any remaining issues.

Summary:

The hearing was requested on May 23, 2012, with the original mailing date for the ﬁnal decision
and order being August 3, 2012. Mediation was requested by the paltiest An extension of the
deadline was requested in writing, and submitted to the Hearing Ofﬁcer and all parties. The
request was made for good cause shown, and was therefore GRANTED. A thirty day extension
of the resolution period extended the resolution period until July 20, 2012, and the mailing date
for the F inal Decision and Order was extended to September 3, 2012. Mediation was held and
resolution was reached. On July 30, 2012, the Parents withdrew their request for a Due Process

Hearing, with prejudice.

Final Decision and Order:

The matter is dismissed with prejudice.
