FINAL DECISION AND ORDER

ISSUES:

1. Did the Board provide FAPE to student for the two years prior to the ﬁling of the Due
Process Complaint?

2. Did the Board offer FAPE for the 2015-2016 school year?

3. If not, is EHSS [Eagle H111 School w Southport] an appropriate placement for Student?

4. If so, should the Board be required to reimburse Parents for payment of tuition and
education related expenses at EHSS?

5. Do the circumstances warrant an award of compensatory education?

PROCEDURAL HISTORY/SUMMARY:

The Parent ﬁled the Due Process Complaint and Request for Hearing on September 2,
2015. The Hearing Ofﬁcer conducted a Prehearing Conference on September 18, 2015.
The parties’ request to extend the mailing date to engage in mediation was granted and the
hearing was scheduled for December 9, 2015. On December 7, 2015, the Parent’s attorney
reported to the Hearing Ofﬁcer that the Parent was withdrawing the Due Process Complaint
without prejudice.

FINAL DECISION AND ORDER:

The matter is DISMISSED without prejudice.
