FINAL DECISION AND ORDER

ISSUES:

1. Did the District fail to provide Student with a Free Appropriate Public Education
(“FAPE”) for the 2014—2015 school year?

2. Did the District fail to provide Student with a FAPE for the Extended School Year during
the summer of 2015?

3. Did the District fail to provide Student with a FAPE for 2015-2016 school year?

PROCEDURAL HISTORY AND SUMMARY:

On August 25, 2015, the Board received Student’s request for a Special Education Due Process
Hearing, and the undersigned Hearing Ofﬁcer was appointed. During the preheating conference
held on September 8, 2015, hearing dates of October 29, 2015 and November 3, 2015 were set
and the deadline for mailing the Final Decision and Order was established as November 6, 2015.
On September 18, 2015, the Attorney for the Student withdrew Student’s request for a Special
Education Due Process Hearing with prejudice.

FINAL DECISION AND ORDER:

In light of the above facts, the case is dismissed.
