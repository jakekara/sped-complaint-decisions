FINAL DECISION AND ORDER

ISSUES:

1. Did the Board fail to provide Student with a Free Appropriate Public Education in the Least
Restrictive Environment for the 2012-2013 school year?

2. Does the program proposed by the Board for the 2013—2014 school year provide the Student
with a Free Appropriate Public Education in the Least Restrictive Environment?

3. If the Program proposed by the Board for the 2013-2014 school year does not provide the
Student with a Free Appropriate Public Education in the Least Restrictive Environment, is an
out of district placement the appropriate placement for Student?

PROCEDURAL HISTORY AND SUMMARY:

On July 26, 2013, the Greenwich Board of Education received a Request for a Special Education
Due Process Hearing and the undersigned hearing ofﬁcer was appointed. Prehearing conferences
were held on August 9, 2013 and August 26, 2013 and the original deadline for the mailing of
the ﬁnal decision and order was established as October 9, 2013.

Pursuant to discussion during the first prehearing conference, an amended request was ﬁled by
Parent on August 20, 2013, to which the Board had no objection. A new deadline for the mailing
of the ﬁnal decision and order resulted from the amendment. The revised deadline was
established as November 3, 2013. Hearing dates were scheduled for October 15, 2013, October
16, 2013, October 23, 2013 and October 29, 2013 and October 30, 2013. On September 16,
2013, the parties requested that the previously scheduled hearing dates of October 15, 2013 and
October 16, 2013 be postponed so that the parties might engage in mediation. This request was
granted resulting in the initial date of hearing falling on October 23, 2013. On October 21, 2013,

Parents withdrew the hearing request.

FINAL DECISION AND ORDER:

In 11 ght 0f the above facts, the case is dismissed.
