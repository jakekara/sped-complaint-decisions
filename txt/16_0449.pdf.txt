PROCEDURAL HISTORY AND SUMMARY:

On April 5, 2016, the Board received a request for a special education due process hearing ﬁled
by Student. On April 7, 2016, the Connecticut State Depaltment of Education appointed the
undersigned Hearing Ofﬁcer to preside over the case. During the prehearing conference held on
April 11, 2016, hearing dates OfJune 16, 2016, June 27, 2016, July 1, 2016 and July 15, 2016
were set and the deadline for mailing the Final Decision and Order was established as June 17,
2016. On June 3, 2016, after the parties engaged in a successful mediation, Attomey for the
Parents withdrew Student’s hearing request with prejudice.

FINAL DECISION AND ORDER:

In light of the above facts, the case is dismissed.
