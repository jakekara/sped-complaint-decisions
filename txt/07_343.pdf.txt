 By letter dated November 5, 2007 to the 
State of Connecticut, Department of 
Education (&#64257;CTDOE&#64258;) Bureau of Special Education, the Parents commenced this due

e date established for the mailing of the 
Final Decision and Order.  The parties participated in a CTDOE-sponsored mediation on 
December 12, 2007.  The parties thereafter repor

through mediation to resolve their dispute and 
that this matter was 
being withdrawn with prejudice.

order are hereby terminated and this matter is hereby DISMISSED.
