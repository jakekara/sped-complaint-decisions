F INAL DECISION AND ORDER

 

ISSUES:
1. Did the Board provide the Student a FAPE in the LRE for the 2010-2011 school
year?
2. If not, what relief is appropriate?
3. Did the Board provide the Student a FAPE in the LRE for the 201 1—2012 school
year?
4. If not, what relief is appropriate?
5. Has the Board proposed an appropriate IEP for the 2012—2013 school year?
6. If not, is the Parent’s proposed placement appropriate and reimbursable?
SUMMARY:

A special education hearing in the above-captioned matter was requested by the Student’s
attorney and received by the Board on August 8, 2012. On August 20, 2012, Attorney Spencer
appeared on behalf of the Parents and Attorney Yoder on behalf of the Board in a telephonic pre—
hearing conference. On August 22, 2012, in accordance with the terms of a settlement
agreement between the patties, the Parents withdrew the Request for Due Process.

FINAL DECISION AND ORDER:

The matter is DISMISSED with prejudice.
