  FINAL DECISION AND ORDER  The prehearing on this matter was conducted on August 27, 2004 and the first hearing 
date was set for September 10, 2004 with a date for mailing of the final decision of 
October 4, 2004.  At the first day of hearing the Board counsel reported that the matter 
had been worked out in a long mediation the day before but had not yet been reduced to 
writing and finalized.  At that time the Board requested, by agreement of the parties, that 
the previously scheduled hearing date of September 13, 2004 be retained.  At the hearing 

of September 13, 2004 the Director reported that the matter had been settled. 
 FINAL DECISION AND ORDER:  The hearing is dismissed without prejudice.
