  FINAL DECISION AND ORDER  The prehearing conference in this matter was held on September 13, 2004 and the parties 
agreed to an extension of the date for mailing of the final decision to January 2, 2005. 
The hearing was set to begin on December 2, 2004, at which time the parties requested 
time to negotiate a settlement and this was granted.  They reported back that they wanted 
a continuance to finalize the settlement.  This was granted with the condition that the 
matter would be dismissed without prejudice as of December 17, 2004 unless the parties 

notified the hearing officer that the matter should be dismissed with prejudice.   
 FINAL DECISION AND ORDER  The matter is 
DISMISSED.
