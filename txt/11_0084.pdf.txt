SUMMARY: On August 13, 2010, the Board received the Request
 for Due Process. On August 30, 2010, a Prehearing Conference was held and hearing dates were scheduled. On September 14, 2010, Counsel for the Parents requested 
postponement of the hearing and extension of the deadline for mailing the Final Decision and Order. That request 

was granted and the Deadline extended to November 26, 2010. On October 25, 2010, counsel for the Parent 

withdrew the Request for Due Process with prejudice.   
FINAL DECISION AND ORDER:
  The matter is
