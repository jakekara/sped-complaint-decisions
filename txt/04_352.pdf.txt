   FINAL DECISION AND ORDER SUMMARY  The student is an almost 19 years old young man diagnosed with pervasive 
developmental disabilities who is classified with Multiple Disabilities and eligible for 
special education and related services as defined in the Individuals With Disabilities 
Education Act (&#64257;IDEA&#64258;) 20 U.S.C. &#167;&#167;1401 et seq. At a PPT meeting the parents 
requested that the Board reimburse them for cost for residential placement for the school 
year 2001-2002 (from February 18, 2002), school year 2002-2003 and for the 2004-2005 

school year. The Board refused to reimburse the parents and the parents on November 11, 
2004 requested a Due Process Hearing. A pre-hearing conference was held November 19, 
2004. A hearing date was scheduled for January 7, 2005. The Final Order and Decision date was extended to February 3, 2005 at the request of the parents because the hearing date is beyond the original filing date of the final order and decision. On or about January 5, 2005 a facsimile transmission was received by the Hearing 

Officer stating that the parties had reach an amicable agreement. The parents&#8482; attorney 
also requested that the matter be dismissed with prejudice. 
 FINAL DECISION AND ORDER  The matter is Dismissed with Prejudice.
