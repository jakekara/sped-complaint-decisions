  FINAL DECISION AND ORDER   The prehearing in this matter was conducted on May 23, 2005 and both parties 

participated.  The Parent was the moving party in this matter and the issues included 
extended year services.  The original date for mailing of the final decision was July 1, 
2005.  The Parties provided records and agreed that the forty five days for mailing the 
final decision could be extended so that the parties could pursue mediation.  Prior to the 
first date of hearing the parties reported that the matter had been resolved. 
 FINAL DECISION AND ORDER  The matter is 
DISMISSED.
