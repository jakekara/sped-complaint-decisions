F INAL DECISION AND ORDER

ISSUES:

1. Whether the Board’s program for the Student for the 2012-2013 school year is
appropriate?

2. If not, whether the Parent’s proposed placement of the Student at Speech Academy or a
similar placement is appropriate?

PROCEDURAL HISTORY/DISCUSSION:

This request for hearing was received by the Board on March 25, 2013 and prehearing
conferences convened on April 1 and April 3, 2013. A resolution session was scheduled for

April 3, 2013.

On April 24, 2013, the Board’s attorney submitted notiﬁcation that the case was settled. The
Board’s attorney and the Parent were notiﬁed that the notice of settlement must be submitted by
the Parent. Subsequently the Parent directly submitted notiﬁcation to the hearing ofﬁcer that she
was withdrawing the case as she has a resolution agreement with the Board. Therefore, this
matter is dismissed.

FINAL DECISION AND ORDER:

The matter is DISMISSED,
