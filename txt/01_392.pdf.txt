Waterbury Board of Education  Appearing on behalf of the Student:          Attorney Alyce Alfano              Klebanoff & Phelan, P.C.              433 South Main Street               Suite 102              West Hartford, CT 06110          Appearing on behalf of the Board of Education:  Attorney Peter Abare-Brown             Office of Corporation Counsel             City of Waterbury             236 Grand Street             Waterbury CT 06702   Appearing before:    Attorney Christine B. Spak, Hearing Officer      FINAL DECISION AND ORDER  ISSUE:  Whether the Board is legally responsible for providing transportation for the Student to and from her placement for the summer of 2000 and the 2001-2002 school year.   SUMMARY:  The prehearing conference in this matter was held on January 3, 2002.  The first day of hearing was set for January 16, 2002 and upon convening the parties requested and were granted time to finalize a stipulation.  This having been done the matter was dismissed with prejudice effective January 16, 2002.   FINAL DECISION AND ORDER:  The matter is DISMISSED with prejudice.
