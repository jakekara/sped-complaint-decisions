FINAL DECISION AND ORDER

ISSUES:

1. Are the Board of Education’s neuropsychological and psychiatric evaluations of the
Student appropriate?

2. If not, is the Student entitled to an Independent Educational Evaluation at public
expense?

PROCEDURAL HISTORY:

The Board of Education requested a special education due process hearing in the above-
captioned matter on March 22, 2016. The Impaitial Hearing Ofﬁcer was appointed to hear the
case on March 29, 2016. A telephonic pre-hearing conference was scheduled for April 21, 2016.
On April 21, 2016, the Board of Education requested that the matter be dismissed.

FINAL DECISION AND ORDER:

It is ordered that the Board of Education’s request for dismissal granted and this matter is
dismissed.
