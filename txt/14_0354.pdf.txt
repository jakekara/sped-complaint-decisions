FINAL DECISION AND ORDER

ISSUES:

1. Was the Board’s physical therapy evaluation appropriate?
2. Is the Board obligated to fund an independent physical therapy evaluation at public
expense?

PROCEDURAL HISTORY/SUMMARY:

The Board brought the Due Process Complaint and Hearing Request on January 29,
2014. The Hearing Ofﬁcer was appointed on January 30, 2014. The Hearing Ofﬁcer
conducted a Prehearing Conference on February 19, 2014 and scheduled a hearing for
March 11, 2014. On February 26, 2014, the Parents informed the Hearing Ofﬁcer that
they were withdrawing their request for an independent educational evaluation for
physical therapy. On February 27, 2014, the Board’s attorney informed the Hearing
Ofﬁcer that the Board was withdrawing its Due Process Complaint and Request for
Hearing in light of the Parents’ withdrawal of their request for an independent
educational evaluation.

FINAL DECISION AND ORDER:

The matter is DISMISSED.
