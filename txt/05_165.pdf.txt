  FINAL DECISION AND ORDER  The prehearing conference in this matter was held on June 27, 2005.  Both sides 
presented issues and these included payment for various placements and an evaluation, 
permission to conduct a triennial evaluation, and transition services.  The parties agreed 
to hearing dates and to an extension of the 45 days to mail the final decision.  The date 
for mailing of the final decision was extended to September 2, 2005.  Records were 
provided but prior to the first day of hearing the Parent withdrew their request for reason 
of a serious illness in the family.  
  FINAL DECISION AND ORDER:  The hearing is dismissed.
