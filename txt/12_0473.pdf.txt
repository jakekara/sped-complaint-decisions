FINAL DECISION AND ORDER

ISSUES:

1. Whether the Board’s proposed program for the Student for the 2012~13 school year is
appropriate;

2. If not, whether the Guardian’s requested out of district placement at a placement such as
CBS in Trumbull is appropriate.

PROCEDURAL HISTORY/DISCUSSION:

The request for hearing was received by the Board on June 5, 2012, and a prehearing conference
convened on June 12, 2012. The resolution session convened on June 13, 2012‘

On June 14, 2012 the Guardian and the Uncle submitted notiﬁcation that the matter was resolved

at the resolution session, and that the Guardian would like to withdraw the hearing request.
Therefore, this matter is dismissed.

FINAL DECISION AND ORDER:

The matter is DISMISSED.
