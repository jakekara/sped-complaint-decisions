  FINAL DECISION AND ORDER  PROCEDURAL HISTORY   The Parent (Father) filed a due process hearing request with the State Department of 
Education (SDE) on March 31, 2006.  This Hearing Officer was assigned to the case on April 4.  On April 17, the Board's attorneys filed an appearance and a response to the due 
process complaint.  The Board received the due process request on April 5, 2006. A 
prehearing conference was scheduled for April 25.  On April 20 the Board's director of 
Pupil Personnel Services sent a letter to the Hearing Officer stating that the matter was 
resolved on April 19 between the Board, the Parents and their attorney.  There was no appearance by the Parents' attorney filed w
ith the Hearing Officer.  On April 21, the Hearing Officer contacted the SDE and was advised that an agreement signed by the 
parties was sent to the SDE on April 19.  On April 24, the Board's attorney wrote to the 
Hearing Officer requesting a cancellation of the prehearing conference and attached a copy of the resolution agreement.  The decision deadline is June 19, 2006. 
 FINAL DECISION AND ORDER  It is ordered that this case is dismissed.
