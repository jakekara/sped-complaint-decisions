      FINAL DECISION  
 The prehearing conference was conducted on August 18, 2006.  Both original parties (the Student and Windsor Locks) part
icipated and the Student agreed
 to an extension of time for mailing of the final decision to accommodate the 
parties&#8482; desire to proceed to mediation and 
attempt to settle the matter.  Subsequent to 
the prehearing the Student filed a motion to add 
Suffield as a party and that was granted.  The issues included evaluation and placement.  At the 
second day of hearing the parties were successful 
in their attempts to resolve the matter and 
agreed that the matter should be
 dismissed with prejudice. 
 ORDER  The matter is 
DISMISSED WITH PREJUDICE.
