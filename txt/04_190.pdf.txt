      FINAL DECISIONAND ORDER
   The prehearing conference in this matter was held on June 28, 2004.  Both parties 
participated and hearing dates were selected.  The parties agreed to participate in mediation and hearing dates were selected to allow for this.  On June 14, 2004 the parties 
requested a cancellation of the June 16, 2004 hearing date and this was not granted.  At the June 16, 2004 hearing the parties requested time to finalize the agreement they had 
reached and this was granted resulting in the agreement being finalized that day.  
  FINAL DECISION AND ORDER:  The hearing is dismissed.
