FINAL DECISION AND ORDER

ISSUES:

1. Did the Board of Education offer the Student 3 free appropriate public education for

the 2011—2012 and/or 2012—2013 School Years?

2. If not, is The Academy of the Holy Family appropriate for the Student? ;
3. If not, what remedies is the Student entitled to, including reimbursement for her

placement, evaluations, transportation and/or compensatory education?

PROCEDURAL HISTORY:

The Student requested a special education hearing in the above-captioned matter on
November 26, 2012. This Impartial Hearing Ofﬁcer was appointed to the case on
November 27, 2012. A telephonic pre-hearing conference was held on December 10,
2012. At that time, hearings were scheduled for March 14 and March 15, 2013.

On December 1 1, 2012, Counsel for the Student made a written request for a thirty—day
postponement and extension of the timelines to conduct the hearing and to ﬁle the ﬁnal

decision in this case to March 6, 2013. That request was granted and the timelines were
extended to March 6, 2013.

On February 6, 2013 Counsel for the Student notiﬁed the Impartial Hearing Ofﬁcer that
the parties had settled the matter and requested that the matter be dismissed.

FINAL DECISION AND ORDER:

It is ordered that the Student’s request for dismissal is granted, and this case is dismissed.
