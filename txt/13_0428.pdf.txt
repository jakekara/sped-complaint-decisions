FINAL DECISION AND ORDER
ISSUES

1. Is the Board’s psychological evaluation of the Student appropriate?

2. If not, are the parents entitled to an independent educational evaluation at the Board’s
expense?

3. Is the Board’s occupational therapy evaluation of the Student appropriate?

4. If not, are the parents entitled to an independent educational evaluation at the Board’s
expense?

SUMMARY

A special education hearing was requested by the Board and received by the Student’s Attorney
on April 16, 2013. Attorney Laviano appeared on behalf of the Student and Attorney Laubin on
behalf of the Board during a telephonic pre-hearing conference held on April 26, 2013. On May
30, 2013, the Board withdrew its request for due process.

FINAL DECISION AND ORDER

The matter is DISMISSED.
