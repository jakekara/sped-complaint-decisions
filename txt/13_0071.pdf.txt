FINAL DECISION AND ORDER

ISSUES:

1. Whether the Board failed to provide/offer FAPE to the Student for the 2010-11, 2011-12

and 2012-13 school years;
2. Whether the Parents’ unilateral placement of the Student at Carmel Academy with related

services is appropriate;
3. Whether the Student is entitled to compensatory education.

PROCEDURAL HISTORY/DISCUSSION:
This request for hearing was received by the Board on August 3, 2012 and a prehearing
conference convened on August 21, 2012. The mailing date of the decision was extended at the

request of the Board’s attorney to provide the parties with additional time to attempt to settle this
case through mediation.

On October 18, 2012, prior to the ﬁrst hearing date, the Parents’ attorney submitted notiﬁcation
that a settlement agreement had been signed and that they were withdrawing this case with
prejudice. Therefore, this matter is dismissed.

FINAL DECISION AND ORDER:

The matter is DISMISSED,
