Hearing Officer   FINAL DECISION AND ORDER  ISSUE:  Whether the Board shall provide a male paraprofessional aide to assist the Student. 
 SUMMARY:  This matter was assigned on August 23, and a prehearing conference was held on 
August 25.  The hearing convened on September 20.  At the hearing on September 7, the 
parties reported on the record that they had tentatively settled the matter.  The hearing 
was continued to October 13.  At the October 13 hearing, the Board attorney reported 
that the Board representative who was needed to finalize the settlement was not present 
due to a medical issue.  The hearing was continued to October 20.  On October 19, the 
Parent&#8482;s attorney submitted a notice that the settlement agreement was finalized.  The 

October 20 hearing was cancelled.  FINAL DECISION AND ORDER:  The matter is 
DISMISSED, with prejudice.
