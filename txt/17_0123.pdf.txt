FINAL DECISION AND ORDER
ISSUES:

1. Did the June 30, 2016 Individualized Education Program (“IEP”) fail to provide Student
with a Free Appropriate Public Education?

2. If yes, was Student’s unilateral placement at Eagle Hill an appropriate placement?

3. If yes, should Parents be reimbursed for tuition at Eagle Hill School for the 2016-2017
school year?

PROCEDURAL HISTORY AND SUMMARY:

On August 31, 2016, the Board received a request for a special education due process hearing
that was ﬁled by Attorney for the Student. On September 7, 2016, the Connecticut State
Depaxtment of Education appointed this Hearing Ofﬁcer to preside over the case. During the
prehearing conference held on September 19, 2016, a hearing date of October 18, 2016 was
scheduled and the deadline for mailing the ﬁnal decision and order was established as November
14, 2016. On October 5, 2016, the Attorney for the Parents withdrew the request for a special
education due process hearing without prejudice.

FINAL DECISION AND ORDER:

In light of the above facts, the case is dismissed.
