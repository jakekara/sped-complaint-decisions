      FINAL DECISION AND ORDER 
  The prehearing conference in this matter was conducted on October 6, 2004 at which 

time hearing dates were scheduled by mutual agreement.  The date for mailing of the 
final decision was extended by agreement to December 23, 2004.  At the first day of 
hearing on November 22, 2004 the parties requested time to pursue settlement 
negotiations and this was granted.  They reported back that they had reached agreement 
in principle and needed additional time to finalize the matter.  This was granted with the 

understanding that if a settlement was reached the matter would be dismissed with 
prejudice.  By subsequent letter the parent&#8482;s counsel reported that they had reached an agreement. 
  FINAL DECISION AND ORDER  The matter is 
DISMISSED WITH PREJUDICE.
