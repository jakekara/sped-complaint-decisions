  FINAL DECISION AND ORDER  The prehearing in this matter was conducted on June 6, 2005 and both parties 

participated.  The Board was the moving party in this matter.  The Board Director 
advised that they would be retaining counsel for the hearing.  The parties agreed to a hearing date of June 17, 2005 and agreed to provide records for review.  The date for mailing of the final decision was July 15, 2005 and it was not extended. The issues 
included evaluation of the child who was not at the time of the hearing request identified 
as a child in need of special education services.   Subsequent to the prehearing the parties advised that they agreed would be participating in a mediation.  At the first day of 
hearing a subsequent hearing date was selected by the parties which would allow the matter to be completed within the original forty-five days.  Prior to the second date of 
hearing the parties reported that the matter had been resolved. 
 FINAL DECISION AND ORDER  The matter is 
DISMISSED.
