FINAL DECISION AND ORDER
ISSUES:

1. Were the Board’s 2014 reevaluations appropriate?

2. If not, are Parents entitled to an independent neuropsychological evaluation at public expense?

3. Was the program offered by the Board for the 2015—2016 school year appropriate and does it
provide the Student with a free and appropriate public education (FAPE) in the least restrictive
environment (LRE)? If not;

4. Should the Board place the Student at Watkinson School for the remainder of the 2015-2016
school year at public expense?

5. Did the Board predetermine the Student’s program prior to the planning and placement team
(PPT) meeting?

6. Did the Board allow the Parents to be meaningful participants of the PPT?

7. Should the Board reimburse the Parents for out of pocket expenses?

8. Is the Student entitled to compensatory education for the denial of FAPE?
