PROCEDURAL HISTORY/SUMMARY:

The Surrogate Parent ﬁled the Due Process Complaint and Request for Hearing on
September 10, 2014. The Hearing Ofﬁcer was appointed on September 11, 2014 and
conducted a Prehearing Conference on September 22, 2014. At the Prehearing Conference,
the Hearing Ofﬁcer issued an order of Stay Put in response to a request by the Surrogate
Parent. The hearing was scheduled for October 22, 2014. On October 6, 2014, the parties
jointly requested a postponement of the hearing date in order to obtain an Advisory
Opinion on this matter. The parties’ request was granted and the hearing date was
postponed to November 19, 2014. The Advisory Opinion was assigned to Hearing Ofﬁcer
Ann Bird. On November 3, 2014, the Surrogate Parent reported to Hearing Ofﬁcer Bird
that the panties had settled their dispute and that the Surrogate Parent was withdrawing the
Due Process Complaint with prejudice.
