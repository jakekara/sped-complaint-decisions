ISSUE:

Is the Connection Program at Wheeler School appropriate and does it provide the
Student with a free and appropriate program in the least restrictive environment?

FINAL DECISION AND ORDER

SUMMARY and PROCEDURAL HISTORY:

The Student has been identiﬁed with Multiple Disabilities and is entitled to receive
a free and appropriate public education (“FAPB”) as deﬁned in the IDEA 20 U.S.C.
§1401 et seq. and Connecticut General Statute §10-76a. At a PPT meeting, the Board
decided to place the Student at the Connection Program at Wheeler School. The Parent
refused the placement. The Board ﬁled for a due process hearing.

An impanial hearing ofﬁcer was appointed on December 13, 2010 and a pre-
hearing conference was held on December 20, 2010. Hearing dates of January 27, 2011,
February 7 and 8, 201 Iwere chosen by the parties. The parties had a mediation date of
January 13, 201 1 but due to inclement weather, the mediation did not take place. The
parties were able to come to an agreement on the matter and request a withdrawal without
prejudice. The request was granted. The mailing date for the Final Decision and Order is

February 21, 2011.
FINAL DECISION AND ORDER:

The matter is DISMISSED without prejudice.
