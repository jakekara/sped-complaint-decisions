F INAL DECISION AND ORDER
ISSUES:

1. Whether the Board failed to properly identify the Student as eligible for special education from
July 1, 2011‘to December 13, 2011;

2. Whether the Board failed to offer the Student a program which provided him with a free
appropriate public education [FAPE] from December 13, 201 1 to the end of the 201 1-12

school year;
3. Whether the Board failed to offer the Student FAPE for the 2012-13 and 2013-14 school years

and ESY 2013;
4. Whether the Parents’ placement of the Student at Aspire Vantage Point Wilderness Program

from January 15, 2013 to March 27, 2013 was appropriate and shall be reimbursed;

5. Whether the Parents’ placement of the Student at Discovery Ranch from March 27, 2013 to the
end of the 2012—13 school year was appropriate and shall be reimbursed;

6. Whether the Discovery Ranch is an appropriate placement for the Student for ESY 2013 and

the 2013—14 school year;
7. Whether the Board shall reimburse the Parents for the educational evaluation and placement

evaluation by Dr. Laura Seese;
8. Whether the Student is entitled to compensatory education.

PROCEDURAL HISTORY/DISCUSSION:

This hearing request was received by the Board on July 1, 2013, and a preheating conference convened
on July 8, 2013. At the preheating conference the attorneys for both patties conﬁrmed that the patties

had agreed to mediate this case.

The hearing convened on August 20, 2013 and continued to October 3, 2013. On October 1, 2013,
prior to the second hearing date, the Parents’ attorney submitted notiﬁcation that a settlement
agreement had been reached and requested to withdraw the request for due process. Therefore, this

matter is dismissed.
FINAL DECISION AND ORDER:

The matter is DISMISSED.
