F INAL DECISION AND ORDER

ISSUES:

1. Whether the Board’s program for the Student for the 2011-12 school year is appropriate.

2. Whether the Board’s program for the Student for the 2010—1 1 school year was appropriate.

3. Whether the Board’s program for the Student for the 2009-10 school year, from Dec. 1, 2009 to
the end of the school year, was appropriate.

4. Whether the Board has committed procedural violations which have resulted in the denial of
FAPE to the Student from December 1, 2009 to the 201 1-12 school year.

5. Whether the Student shall be placed at a school such as High Roads or the CCMC school as
requested by the Parent.

6‘ Whether the Student is entitled to two years of compensatory education.

PROCEDURAL HISTORY/DISCUSSION:
This request for hearing was received by the Board on December 1, 2012 and a preheating conference

convened on December 7, 2012. The parties agreed to mediate the case, which was convened on
January 12, 2012.

On January 13, 2012 prior to the hearing date of January 18, 2012 the Parents’ attorney submitted
notiﬁcation that the case was settled. Therefore, this matter is dismissed.

FINAL DECISION AND ORDER:

The matter is DISMISSED.
