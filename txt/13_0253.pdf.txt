ISSUES:

1. Has the Board failed to offer the Student a free appropriate public education
(“FAPE”) for the 2012-2013 school year?

SUMMARY:

A special education due process hearing was requested by Attorney Halm and received by the
Board on December 11, 2012, Attorney Halm appeared on behalf of the Student, and Attorney
Kaufmann on behalf of the Board, during a telephonic pre-hearing conference on January 3,
2013. On February 6, 2013, Attorney Hahn withdrew the request for a due process hearing.

FINAL DECISION AND ORDER:

The matter is DISMISSED.
