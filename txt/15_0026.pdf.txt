PROCEDURAL HISTORY/SUMMARY:
The Parent ﬁled the Due Process Complaint and Request for Hearing on July 1, 2014 and
the Board received it on July 15, 2014. The Hearing Ofﬁcer was appointed on July 15,

Ofﬁcer that the Parent was withdrawing the Due Process Complaint without prejudice.

FINAL DECISION AND ORDER:
