FINAL DECISION AND ORDER

ISSUES:

1.

Were the Individualized Education Programs (IEPS) and placements offered by the
Board for school years 2008-2009, 2009-2010, and 2010-201 1 appropriate to the
Student’s special education needs in the least restrictive environment?

Did the Student require direct, regularly scheduled related services of
Speech/Language Therapy (S/L) and Occupational Therapy (OT) during 2008-2009,
2009-2010 and 2010-2011?

Did the Board fail to provide a triennial re-evaluation in 2010?
Are Parents entitled to an independent S/L evaluation of the Student?

Did the Board deprive Student’s Parents of their right to participate in the Flaming
and Placement Team (PPT) process?

Are the Parents entitled to reimbursement from the Board for documented evaluations
and related services provided at Parents’ expense?

Note: possible reimbursement limited to two years, from April 6, 2009, pursuant to
Section 10—76h—4, Regulations of Connecticut State Agencies.

SUMMARY:

The Parents challenged the appropriateness of 2008-2009, 2009-2010, and 2010—2011
Individualized Education Programs and placements provided by the Board. They
claimed that related services needed by Student were not provided. They requested
reimbursement for evaluations and related services they had provided. The case was
settled in mediation, and the Parents withdrew their request for a hearing.

members and other witnesses in place of names and other personally identifiable

information.

CONCLUSIONS OF LAW:

Section 10-76h, Connecticut General Statutes (C.G.S.), and related regulations at Section
10-76h, Regulations of Connecticut State Agencies, authorize an impartial hearing ofﬁcer
to conduct a special education hearing and to render a ﬁnal decision in accordance with
Sections 4—176e through 4—180a, inclusive, and Sections 4—1 81a 0fthe C.G.S. Section 20
U.S.C. § 1415(0 and related regulations at 34 CPR. § 300.511 through § 300.520 also
authorize special education hearings.

FINAL DECISION AND ORDER:

The dispute having been settled and the request for hearing withdrawn, this case is
DISMISSED.
