 FINAL DECISION AND ORDER 
 
 ISSUES:   
 1. Whether the Board&#8482;s proposed program fo
r the Student for the summer 2007 and 
the 2007-2008 school year is appropriate. 2. If not, whether the Parents&#8482; residential placement of the Student at Devereux 

Center effective July 2 is appropriate.   3. Whether the Student requires a residential placement to receive a free appropriate 
public education [FAPE].   SUMMARY: 
 The Board received this request for hearing on July 17, and a prehearing 
conference was held on August 2.  At the first hearing date on September 14, it was 
reported that the Parents were attempting to 
have another agency participating in the placement, providing alternative funding.  Th
e case was continued to October 23, which 
was postponed to November 5 to allow the parties to coordinate with the other agency.    
At the hearing on November 5, the Board&#8482;s attorney reported that the matter was 
resolved, and submitted a facsimile from the Parents&#8482; attorney which confirmed that the 

parties had just reached a settlement of the matter.   
 FINAL DECISION AND ORDER:
  
 The matter is 
DISMISSED.
