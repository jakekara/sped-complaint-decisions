Hearing Officer       FINAL DECISION AND ORDER
    The prehearing conference in this matter was held on February 23, 2004.  Over objection 
from the Board, who initially had different counsel, the first day of hearing was set for 
March 1, 2004, at which time testimony of the mother was presented.  At the beginning 
of the second day of hearing the parties requested time to attempt to resolve the matter, 
which was granted.  The parties reported back that the matter had been resolved to their 
mutual satisfaction. 
   FINAL DECISION AND ORDER:  The hearing is dismissed.
