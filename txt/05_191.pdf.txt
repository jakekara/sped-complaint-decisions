  FINAL DECISION AND ORDER  The prehearing in this matter was conducted on July 7, 2005 and both parties 

participated.  The issues included summer programming, the number of hours, the 
intensity of the services, and the length of the summer program previously offered by the 
Board.  The parties requested time to attempt to resolve the matter.  Prior to the hearing 
the Parent advised that the matter had been resolved to her satisfaction and expressed 
appreciation that the Due Process system had insured that the matter had been resolved 
quickly.   FINAL DECISION AND ORDER  The matter is 
DISMISSED.
