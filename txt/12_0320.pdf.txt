FINAL DECISION AND ORDER
ISSUES:

1. Has the Board offered a free, appropriate public education (FAPB) to the Student for the
balance of the 2011-2012 school year?
2. If not, is the educational placement proposed by the Parent appropriate?

SUMMARY:

A special education hearing in the above-captioned matter was requested by the Student’s
attorney and received by the Board on February 29, 2012. On March 5, 2012, a pre—hearing
conference was held and hearing dates were scheduled. On April 2, 2012, the Parent withdrew
the Request for Due Process with prejudice.

FINAL DECISION AND ORDER:

The matter is DISMISSED with prejudice.
