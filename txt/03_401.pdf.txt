Hearing Officer   FINAL DECISION AND ORDER  ISSUES:  1. Was the program offered by the Board for the 2003-2004 school year 
appropriate as required by 20 U.S.C. &#167; 1401 et seq? 
2. Should the student be provided with an extended school year? 
  SUMMARY  The student is a five year old young boy who has bee identified as a student with an autism as defined in by 20 U.S.C. &#167; 1401 et seq, and eligible for special education and 
related services. The parents on June 17, 2003 requested an all day program for the 
student which was refused by the district.. The parents requested a due process hearing on December 23, 2003.  A pre-hearing conference was conducted on December 30, 2003 

and initial hearing dates were agreed. The hearing date chosen was January 26, 2004.  The parent sent a fax transmittal to the hearing officer requesting the cancellation of the 

hearing dates and the withdrawal of the matter as the parties had reached an agreement. 
  FINAL DECISION AND ORDER  The matter is 
DISMISSED, with prejudice.
