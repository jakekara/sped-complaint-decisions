FINAL DECISION AND ORDER

ISSUES:

1, Whether or not the Board’s Individualized Education Program for the 201 1-2012 school year
provided the Student with a free appropriate public education;

2. Whether or not the Board’s decision not to provide an extended school year program for the
Student during the summer of 2012 resulted in a denial of a free appropriate public education;

3. Whether or not the Board’s Individualized Education Program for the 2012-2013 school year
provides the Student with a free appropriate public education;

4. If the program offered by the Board for the 2012—2013 school year does not provide the
Student with a free appropriate public education, what is the appropriate program for the
Student?

SUMMARY:

On August 10, 2012, the Bristol Board of Education received a Request for a Special Education
Due Process Hearing. The undersigned hearing ofﬁcer was appointed on August 13, 2012. On
August 24, 2012 a preheating conference was held and hearing dates were set The deadline for
the mailing of the Final Decision and Order was October 24, 2012. The paﬂies engaged in
mediation on September 4, 2012 and reached an agreement. On September 1 1, 2012, Counsel
for the Student withdrew the hearing request with prejudice.

FINAL DECISION AND ORDER:

In light 0fthe above facts, the case is dismissed with prejudice.
