SUMMARY AND PROCEDURAL HISTORY:

The Student has been identiﬁed as Other Health Impaired (01-11) and is entitled to receive FAPE as
deﬁned in the Individuals With Disabilities Education Improvement Act (IDEA) 20 U.S.C. §1401 et
seq. and Connecticut General Statute §10-76a et seq. At a planning and placement team (PPT) meeting,
the Parents rej ected the program provided by the Board for the 2012—2013 school year. The Parents
requested compensatory education for the denial of FAPE. The Board refused the Parents request.
