Appearing before: Attorney Elisabeth Borrino, Hearing Officer FINAL DECISION AND ORDER ISSUES: 1. Whether the Board failed to provide FAPE for the 2008-2009, and 2009-2010 school years; 
2. Whether the Board failed to offer FAPE for the 2010-2011 school year; 
3. Whether the Student should be placed at the Forman School; 
4. If placed at the Forman School, whether the Board should provide transportation; 
5. Whether the Board should provide compensatory education. 
SUMMARY: On June 22, 2010, the Board received the Request for Due Process.  On July 19, 2010, a Prehearing 
Conference was held and hearing dates were scheduled. On July 26, 2010, Counsel for the Parent withdrew the 
Request for Due Process and requested that the matter be dismissed with prejudice.   
FINAL DECISION AND ORDER: The matter is 
DISMISSED with prejudice.
