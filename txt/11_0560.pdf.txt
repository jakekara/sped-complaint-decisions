PROCEDURAL HISTORY:

This hearing was requested by the Board of Education on July 1, 201 1. The Hearing
Ofﬁcer had been appointed to hear a prior case concerning this student, and she agreed to
consolidate the two cases The date for mailing the decision was September 12, 201 1. A
pre-hearing conference was held on July 19, 201 1 and the hearing was scheduled for
September 6 and 9, 201 1.

Following some confusion about multiple requests for hearings from both Parties
concerning the Student and two siblings, on July 25, 201 1 the Hearing Ofﬁcer issued a
memorandum conﬁrming the consolidation of two cases concerning the Student.

On July 28, 201 1, the Hearing Ofﬁcer received a request to postpone the hearing because

October 12, 201 1. On September 5, 201 1, Parent withdrew her request for a hearing. On

requested an additional postponement due to pending surgery. The Hearing Ofﬁcer re-
scheduled the hearing for December 14, 201 1 and extended the decision date to the same
date. On December 2, 2011, the Board withdrew its request for a hearing.

All motions and objections not previously ruled upon, ifany, are hereby overmled.

SUMMARY:

This hearing was requested by the Board to override the Parents’ refusal to consent to
evaluations of the Student.
