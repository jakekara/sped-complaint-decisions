Mangs   PROCEDURAL HISTORY:     This hearing was requested by the Parent on January 4, 2002.  A prehearing conference was convened on January 17, 2002.  The Parent participated in the conference but the Board of Education representative did not appear.  The Parent informed the hearing officer that she was withdrawing her request for the hearing as she had reached an agreement with the Board of Education.  The hearing officer requested that the Parent send a written withdrawal.  The next day, the hearing officer also mailed the request to the Parent and stated that the hearing would be dismissed on January 25, 2002 if a written withdrawal was not received.  As of the close of business on January 25, 2002, the hearing officer had not received a written withdrawal from the Parent.  FINAL DECISION AND ORDER:  This hearing is dismissed.
