Hearing Officer   FINAL DECISION AND ORDER  This hearing was requested by the Board.  The issue was whether the Board&#8482;s assistive technology evaluation was appropriate, and if not, whether the Board should be required 
to fund an independent assistive technology evaluation.  Hearing dates were selected that were agreed to by the parties.  On the first day of hearing the parties requested more time 
to attempt to negotiate a settlement and this time was given.  Prior to the second date of  

hearing the Board notified the hearing officer that the parties had reached a tentative 
settlement and would not be needing further hearing dates.  
 FINAL DECISION AND ORDER:  The hearing is dismissed.
