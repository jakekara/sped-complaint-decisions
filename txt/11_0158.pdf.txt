ISSUES: 1. Whether the Board failed to identify the Student 
as eligible for Special Education from February 2007 
through September 2010; 
2. Whether the Board denied FAPE for the two years preceding the filing of the Request for Due 
Process; 3.Whether the Student requires a private day placement; 
4. If the Student requires a private day placement 
whether the Student should be placed at Ben Bronz 
Academy with services that include speech and language as well as occupational therapy at the 
expense of the Board; 5.Whether the Board should provide compensatory education. 
SUMMARY: On October 12, 2010, the Board received the Request 
for Due Process.  On October 27, 2010, a Prehearing 
Conference was held and hearing dates were scheduled.  On
 November 8, 2010, Counsel notified the Hearing Officer 
that the parties had reached a settlement agreement, wit
hdrew the Request for Due Process, and requested that the 
matter be dismissed with prejudice.  
FINAL DECISION AND ORDER: 
The matter is DISMISSED with prejudice
