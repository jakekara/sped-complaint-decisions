The Parent requested that the Student be identified 
as learning disabled and eligible to receive special education and related service as defined under IDEA. The Board refused the Parent&#8482;s request and the Parent requested a Due Process Hearing on March 14, 2007 and gave notice to the Board of the request on or about March 15, 2007.  On March 15, 2007, a hearing officer was appointed, a pre-hearing conference was held 
on March 27, 2007 and agreed upon hearing dates of April 24 & 25, 2007 was scheduled.  The parties had a mediation session on Apr
il 25, 2007 at which the parties came to an 
agreement.
