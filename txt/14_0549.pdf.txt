FINAL DECISION AND ORDER

ISSUE:

 

Is the Student entitled to an additional year of transition services?

PROCEDURAL HISTORY:

The Student requested a special education due process hearing in the above-captioned matter
on May 20, 2014. This Impartial Hearing Ofﬁcer was assigned to the case on June 3, 2014.
A telephonic pre-hearing conference was conducted on June 9, 2014. Attorney Karyl Lee
Hall appeared on behalf of the Student. Attomey Marsha Moses appeared on behalf of the
Board of Education. Attorney Linda Yoder appeared on behalf of the Norwich Free
Academy. A further conference was scheduled for June 17, 2014.

On June 12, 2014 the Student reported that the parties had resolved the case and requested
that the Impartial Hearing Ofﬁcer dismiss the matter.

FINAL DECISION AND ORDER:

It is ordered that the Student’s request for dismissal is granted and this matter is dismissed.
