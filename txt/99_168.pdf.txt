September 21, 1999 -2- Final Decision and Order 99-168      This hearing was requested on June 30, 1999.  The prehearing conference was held on July 7, 1999.  Hearings scheduled for July 21 and September 2, 1999 were postponed at the request of the parties.  On September 15, 1999, the hearing was convened at which time the parties notified the hearing officer that all issues had been resolved and that a settlement agreement had been reached.  On the basis, both parties requested that the hearing officer dismiss the hearing.  FINAL DECISION AND ORDER:  This hearing is dismissed.
