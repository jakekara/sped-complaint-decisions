  FINAL DECISION AND ORDER  ISSUES:  1. Whether the Board&#8482;s offer of placement in their pre-school program with speech 
support was appropriate to Student&#8482;s special education needs in the least restrictive environment. 
 2. Whether the Board was required to provide speech and language therapy at Student&#8482;s 
day care program, which is located in an adjacent town.
