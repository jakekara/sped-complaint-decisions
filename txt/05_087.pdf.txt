Hearing Officer   FINAL DECISION AND ORDER  SUMMARY:  This matter was assigned on April 11, 2005, and a prehearing conference was 
held on April 15.  On April 18, the attorney for the Parent submitted a request for an 
extension of the mailing date of the decision so that the parties could attempt to settle the 
matter.  That request was granted in accordance with Section 10-76h-9(e) of the 

Connecticut Regulations which provides for a 30-day postponement for settlement 
discussions between the parties.  On May 12, the attorney for the Parents submitted a notice that the Parent is 
having the Student undergo a further assessment by a private provider, and the 

assessment and report will not be completed in time for the scheduled hearing date of 
June 6.  In accordance with Section 10-76h-9(e) of the Connecticut Regulations, if the 
parties are not prepared to go forward with the hearing after the 30 day postponement for 
settlement discussions, the hearing officer shall dismiss the hearing request without 
prejudice.   FINAL DECISION AND ORDER:  The matter is 
DISMISSED, without prejudice.
