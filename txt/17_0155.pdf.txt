FINAL DECISION AND ORDER
ISSUE:

1. Was the District’s Individualized Education Program (“IEP”) for the 2016-2017 school
year appropriate? If the IEP was not appropriate, should Student be placed at Winston
Preparatory Academy?

2. Did the District commit the procedural Violation of predetermining Student’s placement
and if so, did this procedural violation operate to deny Student a Free Appropriate Public
Education (“FAPE”)?
