Hearing Officer   FINAL DECISION AND ORDER  This hearing was requested by the parent for a large number of issues including failure to 
evaluate, damaging the reputation of the student, inappropriate physical restraint of the 
student, failure to program in the least restrictive environment in the summer of 2005 and 

the 2005-2006 school year, and failure to plan an appropriate program for the summer 

2006 through the 2006-2007 school year.  A prehearing was conducted and both parties 
participated.  Hearing dates were selected and then the parties requested that the first date 
be cancelled and that request was granted.  At the second date of hearing the Board 
reported that the matter had been resolved. 
  FINAL DECISION AND ORDER:  The hearing is dismissed.
