FINAL DECISION AND ORDER
ISSUES:

1. Is the program provided by the Board for the 2014-2015 school year appropriate and does it
provide the Student with a free and appropriate public education (FAPE) in the least
restrictive environment (LRE)? If not;

2. Does the Student require an out of district placement in order to receive FAPE in the LRE?
3. Did the Board commit a procedural violation by not allowing the Parents to be meaningful
palticipant of the PPT?

SUMMARY AND PROCEDURAL HISTORY:

The Student has been identiﬁed with Multiple Disabilities and is entitled to receive FAPE as
deﬁned in the Individuals With Disabilities Education Improvement Act (IDEA) 20 U.S.C.
§1401 et seq. and Connecticut General Statute §10-76a et seq. At a planning and placement
team (PPT) meeting, the Parent rejected the program offered by the Board for the 2014-2015
school year and requested an out of district placement. The Board refused the Parent’s request.

On October 22, 2014, the Board received notice of the Parent’s request for due process. The
parties agreed to a resolution meeting. The resolution date was pending. An impartial hearing
ofﬁcer was appointed on October 22, 2014 and a pre-hearing conference was held on October
27, 2014. A December 2, 2014 hearing date was chosen by the parties. The Board advised the
hearing ofﬁcer that they were going to ﬁle a sufﬁciency challenge.

On November 7, 2014, the Parent withdrew the matter without prejudice. There was no objection
by the Board. The mailing date for the Final Decision and Order is Janua1y 10, 2015.

F INAL DECISION AND ORDER:

THE MATTER IS DISMISSED.
