F INAL DECISION AND ORDER

ISSUES:

1. Did the Board of Education offer the Student 21 Free Appropriate Public Education for
the 2013—2014, 2014-2015 and 2015-2016 School Years?

2. If not, is the Student entitled to compensatory education services?
3. If not, is the Student’s unilateral placement appropriate?
4. If so, is the Student entitled to reimbursement for the expense of the unilateral

placement and/or placement at the unilateral placement?
PROCEDURAL HISTORY:
The Student requested a special education due process hearing on November 17, 2015. The
Impaitial Hearing Ofﬁcer was assigned to hear the case on November 17, 2015. A Prehearing

Conference was scheduled for December 3, 2015. On November 20, 2015, the Student
submitted a written request that the matter be dismissed without prejudice.

FINAL DECISION AND ORDER:

It is ordered that the request of the Student for dismissal is granted and this matter is
dismissed without prejudice.
