Hearing Officer   FINAL DECISION AND ORDER  SUMMARY:  This matter was assigned on May 26, 2005, and a prehearing conference was held 
on May 31.  On June 1 and June 10, the attorney for the Parent submitted requests for an 
extension of the mailing date of the decision and cancellation of hearing dates so that the 
parties could attempt to settle the matter.  The request was granted in accordance with 

Section 10-76h-9(e) of the Connecticut Regulations which provides for a 30-day 
postponement for settlement discussions between the parties, and to schedule hearing 
dates as requested by both counsel.  On July 6, the attorney for the Parent submitted a notice that the parties had 
reached a settlement agreement.    
 FINAL DECISION AND ORDER:  The matter is 
DISMISSED.
