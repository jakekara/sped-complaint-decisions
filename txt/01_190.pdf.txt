If not, whether the Board shall provide for an independent evaluation at public expense, and pursuant to the Parents&#8482; request.  PROCEDURAL HISTORY:  The matter was assigned on June 13, 2001. The parties each requested extensions of the mailing date of the decision prior to the conclusion of testimony, which were granted.  The hearing convened on July 11, 2001, and continued on July 18, July 26, August 27 and August 30. The school psychologist Brigitte Duffy, the school social worker Sheri Barnett and the Director of Special Services Thomas DiCorleto testified on behalf of the Board.  Assistant Principal Alan Addley, therapist Marsi Callaghan, psychiatrist Stanley Kapuchinski, consulting teacher Maureen Pearson, and the Parents testified during the Parents&#8482; case.
