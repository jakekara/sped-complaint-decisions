Appearing before: Attorney Elisabeth Borrino, Hearing Officer FINAL DECISION AND ORDER ISSUES: 1. Whether the Board offered FAPE to the Student; 
2. If not, whether the Student should be placed in a residential therapeutic school; 3. Whether the Board should pay for the cost of a residential therapeutic school. 
SUMMARY: On April 5, 2010, the Board received the Request for Due Process. On April 27, 2010, a Prehearing 
Conference was held and hearing dates were scheduled. Thereafter, Counsel for the Parents withdrew the Request for Due Process, and requested that the matter be
 dismissed without prejudice. The Board did not object. 
FINAL DECISION AND ORDER: The matter is 
DISMISSED without prejudice.
