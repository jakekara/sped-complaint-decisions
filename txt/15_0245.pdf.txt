FINAL DECISION AND ORDER
ISSUE:
1. Did the Board of Education offer the Student at free appropriate public education for

the 2013-14 and 2014—15 school years?
2. If not, is the Windward School appropriate for the Student?

3. If so, should the Student be placed at the Windward School?

4. If so, should the Board of Education reimburse the Student for the expenses of the
Windward School?

5. Is the Student entitled to reimbursement for the expense of independent educational

evaluations of Dr. Pote and/or Dr. Blackman?
6. Did the Board of Education perform its Child Find obligation in a timely fashion?
7. Is the Student entitled to compensatory education?
PROCEDURAL HISTORY:

The Student requested a special education due process hearing on November 24, 2014. This
Impartial Hearing Ofﬁcer was assigned to the case on November 24, 2014.

On January 29, 2015, the Student reported that the matter had been resolved and requested
that it be dismissed.

FINAL DECISION AND ORDER:

It is ordered that the Student’s request for dismissal is granted and this matter is dismissed.
