FINAL DECISION AND ORDER

ISSUES:

1. Did the Board of Education provide the Student a free appropriate public education from
March 21, 201 1 t0 the present?

PROCEDURAL HISTORY:

The Student submitted a Request for Impartial Special Education Hearing, which was received
by the Board of Education on March 21, 2013. The Hearing Ofﬁcer was assigned to the case on
March 25, 2013. A telephonic prehearing conference was conducted on April 2, 2013. Counsel
for each party participated in the prehearing conference. Hearing dates of May 3, 2013, May 29,
2013, May 30, 2013, June 12, 2013 and June 14, 2013 were scheduled

On April 25, 2013, Counsel for the Student notiﬁed the Hearing Ofﬁcer that the Student
requested to withdraw her Request for Due Process Hearing without prejudice.
FINAL DECISION AND ORDER:
