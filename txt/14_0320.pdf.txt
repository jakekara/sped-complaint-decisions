FINAL DECISION AND ORDER
ISSUES:

1. Did the Board provide appropriate program, including extended year programs for
the December 16, 2011-2012, 2012-2013 school years?

2. Did the Board offer an appropriate program for the 2013~2014 school year?

3. Did the Board offer an appropriate transition program to student?

4. Is the Learning Incentive Program appropriate?

PROCEDURAL HISTORY/SUMMARY:

The Parent ﬁled the Due Process Complaint and Request for Hearing on December 16,
2013. The Hearing Ofﬁcer was appointed on December 17, 2014 and conducted a
Prehearing Conference on December 24, 2013. The hearing was scheduled for February 24,
2014. On February 12, 2014, the Parent’s attorney repmted to the Hearing Ofﬁcer that the
parties had reached an agreement and that the Parent was withdrawing the Due Process
Complaint without prejudice.

FINAL DECISION AND ORDER:

The matter is DISMISSED without prejudice.
