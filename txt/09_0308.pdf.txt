 FINAL DECISION AND ORDER
  A special education hearing in the above-captioned matter was requested by the Student via 
letter dated January 30, 2009. The request for due
 process hearing was received by the Board of 
Education on January 30, 2009. The 30-day resolution period ran through March 1, 2009. The deadline for mailing the final deci
sion and order is April 15, 2009.  ISSUES:  A pre-hearing conference was held on February 17, 2009 at which the following issues were identified:  1. Did the Board of Education fail to provide Student with a free, appropriate public 
education? 
 2. If the Board of Education did fail to provide Student with a free, appropriate public education, is an out of district private special education placement appropriate? 
 SUMMARY:   The case was scheduled for hearings on Monday, March 30, 2009, and Tuesday, March 31, 2009. The parties participated in a resolution hearing that did not result in a resolution of the matter. 
The parties participated in mediation, which resulte
d in a written agreement to resolve the matter. On 
March 24, 2009, the Student, through its attorney, filed a request to withdraw the request for 
impartial due process hearing. 
 FINAL DECISION AND ORDER
  In light of the above facts, the above-captioned case is hereby dismissed.
