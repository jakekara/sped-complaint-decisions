PROCEDURAL HISTORY/ SUMMARY:

The Board ﬁled the Due Process Request on September 18, 2012. The Hearing Ofﬁcer
was appointed on September 20, 2012. The original mailing date of the Final Decision
was November 3, 2012, A Prehearing Conference was conducted on October 3, 2012
and a hearing was scheduled for November 16, 2012. The mailing date of the F inal
Decision was extended to December 3, 2012. On October 24, 2012, the Board’s attorney
requested a postponement of the ﬁrst day of hearing and the mailing date of the Final
Decision to allow the parties to engage negotiations with a state appointed mediator. The
Board’s request was granted. The hearing date was cancelled and a new hearing date was
scheduled for December 17, 2012. The mailing date of the Final Decision was extended
to December 31, 2012. On December 12, 2012, the Board’s attorney notiﬁed the Hearing
Ofﬁcer that the patties had come to an agreement and that the Board was withdrawing the

Request.
