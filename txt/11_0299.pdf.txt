A special education hearing in the above—captioned matter was requested by the Board of
Education’s (“BOE‘s”) Attorney via letter dated February 1, 2011.1 The deadline to mail the
ﬁnal decision and order is March 17.
The request for hearing identiﬁes the following issues:
1. Is BOE’s November 2010 speech and language evaluation of Student appropriate?
2. If the answer to Issue One is in the negative, are Student’s Parents entitled to an
independent speech and language evaluation at BOE’s expense?
3. Is that portion of BOE’s November 2010 psychological evaluation of Student which
assessed for ADHD appropriate?
4. If the answer to Issue Three is in the negative, are Student’s Parents entitled to an
independent educational evaluation for ADHD deﬁcit at B01373 expense?
A telephonic pre—hearing conference was scheduled for February 23. Via letter dated
J anuary 10 and received by the Hearing Ofﬁcer on February 15, Student’s Parents withdrew their
request for an independent educational evaluation. On February 15, BOE’s Attorney withdrew

the request for hearing.
