Newtown Board of Education     Appearing on behalf of the Parents:   The Parents proceeded pro se.  Appearing on behalf of the Board:   The Board proceeded pro se.  Appearing before:  Attorney Mary Elizabeth Oppenheim, Hearing Officer  FINAL DECISION AND ORDER  ISSUE:  Whether the Parents are entitled to reimbursement for the cost of the Student&#8482;s evaluation.  SUMMARY:  The matter was assigned on June 4, 2002, and a prehearing conference was held on June 6.  The hearing was scheduled for June 25, and the parties were notified of the hearing.  The hearing convened on June 25, with the Board representative present.  The Board representative reported that the case was settled.  FINAL DECISION AND ORDER:  The matter is DISMISSED.
