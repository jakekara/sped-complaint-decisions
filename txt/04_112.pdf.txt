July 6, 2004 -1- Final Decision and Order 04-112   STATE OF CONNECTICUT DEPARTMENT OF EDUCATION    Student  v.  New Hartford Board of Education  Appearing on behalf of the Parents:  Father, pro se  Appearing on behalf of the Board:  Mr. Lawrence W. Niek Director of Special Education and Related Services New Hartford Public Schools 40 Wickett Street New Hartford, CT  06057   Appearing before:    Attorney Patricia M. Strong Hearing Officer   FINAL DECISION AND ORDER   PROCEDURAL HISTORY   The Parent requested this hearing on April 29, 2004.  This hearing officer was assigned to the case on the same date.  A prehearing conference was held on May 6.  At that time, Mr. Niek advised the Hearing Officer that an attorney would not represent the Board and that he would represent the Board.  The parties advised the Hearing Officer that an advisory hearing was scheduled for June 10 and requested a hearing in late June or early July.  A hearing date was agreed on for June 28 and the decision deadline was extended to July 14.  Parties were ordered to file exhibits and witness lists by June 22.  On June 21, the Parent left a telephone message and sent a letter requesting that the time for filing be extended to June 25.  The Parent stated that the parties had reached a tentative agreement, which they anticipated reducing to writing prior to June 25.  No exhibits or withdrawal were received by June 25.  On June 27 the Parent sent a letter asking the Hearing Officer to accept the withdrawal of the "dispute upon settlement between parties."  The parties were advised by letter that the hearing for June 28 was cancelled and the case would be dismissed.  FINAL DECISION AND ORDER  It is ordered that the case be dismissed.
