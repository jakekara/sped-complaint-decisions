FINAL DECISION AND ORDER

ISSUES:
1. Whether the Board shall provide the Student with the program recommended by

the PPT on December 7, 2010;
2. Whether the Student is entitled to compensatory education.

PROCEDURAL HISTORY/DISCUSSION:
This request for hearing was received by the Board on August 24, 201 1 and a prehearing
conference convened on September 7, 201 1. The Board convened a resolution session on

September 6, 201 1 but the Parent’s attorney had a scheduling mistake and did not attend that
session. A second resolution was scheduled.

On September 29, 2011 the Parent’s attorney submitted notiﬁcation that the matter had been
settled between the parties. Therefore, this case is dismissed.

FINAL DECISION AND ORDER:

The matter is DISMISSED.
