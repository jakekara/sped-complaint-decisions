 PROCEDURAL HISTORY  
 The Parents filed a due process hearing request with the Fairfield Board of Education and the State Department of Education (SDE) 
on July 19, 2006.  This Hearing Officer was assigned to the case on July 21.  A prehearing conference was held on August 10.  At the prehearing conference the parties agreed to mediate a
nd two hearing dates were scheduled for September 18 and 26.  The mailing 
date for the decision was set at October 
2, 2006.  The Parents' attorney requested an ex
tension of the decision deadline to October 20, which was granted.  On August 31 the Hear
ing Officer was advised that the parties had not reached agreement at the August 30 me
diation and to proceed with the hearing.  
On September 11, the Parents' attorney wrote 
to the Hearing Officer and stated that the parties had reached an oral agreement to 
settle the matter.  
The case was withdrawn without prejudice pending the execution of a written agreement, which was expected in 
the next several days.  The 
hearings were canceled for September 18 and 26.  No further communications have been received from the parties. 
 FINAL DECISION AND ORDER 
 It is ordered that this case be dismissed.
