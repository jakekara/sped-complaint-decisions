and ESY 2013?

PROCEDURAL HISTORY/DISCUSSION:
The Board received this request for hearing on December 5, 2013 and a preheating conference
convened on December 10, 2013. At the preheating conference the attorneys agreed that a

resolution session would be convened in this matter. The hearing was scheduled to convene on
January 28, 2014.

On January 7, 2014, the Parents’ attorney submitted notiﬁcation that the parties had reached a
settlement in this matter and that the Parents were withdrawing their request for a due process
hearing, with prejudice. Therefore, this matter is dismissed, with prejudice.

FINAL DECISION AND ORDER:

The matter is DISMISSED, with prejudice
