FINAL DECISION AND ORDER

ISSUES

1. Did the Board of Education fulfill its Child Find responsibility for the Student from April
27, 2010 to March 24, 2011 (the date the Student was designated as eligible for special education
and related services)?

2. Did the Board of Education offer the Student a free appropriate public education after
March 24, 2011?

3. If not, is the Merck Center appropriate for the Student?

4. If so, is the Student entitled to reimbursement of the expense of the Merck Center?

5. Is the Student entitled to compensatory education?
