PROCEDURAL HISTORY/SUMMARY:

The Student brought the Due Process Complaint and Request for Hearing on June
10, 2013. The mailing date of the Final Decision is August 23, 2013. The Hearing
Ofﬁcer conducted Prehearing Conferences on June 26, 2013 and June 28, 2013. The

12, 2013. During the hearing the patties informed the Hearing Ofﬁcer that they had
settled this issues in the case. The Student withdrew the Due Process Complaint and
Request for Hearing and asked the Hearing Ofﬁcer to dismiss the matter with prejudice.

FINAL DECISION AND ORDER:

The matter is DISMISSED with prejudice.
