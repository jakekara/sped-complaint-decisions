   FINAL DECISION AND ORDER
  ISSUES:  1.   Whether the School failed to identify the 
Student as eligible for Special Education 
Services; 
 
 2.   Whether the Student should be 
placed at Kildonan School in New York; 
 3.   Whether the Board should reimburse the Parent
s for the all expenses incurred in       placing the Student at Kildonan;  
 4.   Whether there were procedural violations; 

 
 5.   Whether compensatory educati
on should be provided to the Student. 
  SUMMARY:   The Board received this request for hearing filed 
by the Parent on April 21, 2009.  A Pre-Hearing 
Conference was held on May 13, 2009 and June 1, 2009;
 and Hearing Dates were scheduled.  On June 8, 
2009, Counsel for the Parent and Student withdrew the matter without prejudice.   
 FINAL DECISION AND ORDER:
   The matter is 
DISMISSED, without prejudice.
