FINAL DECISION AND ORDER

ISSUES:

1. Whether the Board failed to offer a free, appropriate public education (FAPE) to the Student
for the 2010—201 I school year;

2. Whether the Board failed to offer FAPE to the Student for the 201 1—2012 school year.

SUMMARY:

On February 2, 2012, the Board received the Request for Due Process. On February 10, 2012, a
Prehearing Conference was held and hearing dates were scheduled. On February 16, 2012, the
Parent withdrew the Request for Due Process without prejudice.

FINAL DECISION AND ORDER:

The matter is DISMISSED without prejudice.
