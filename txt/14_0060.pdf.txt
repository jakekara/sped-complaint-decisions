F INAL DECISION AND ORDER
ISSUES:

1. Did the Board of Education offer the Student a free appropriate public education from July 22, 201 1
to the present and for the 2013—2014 School Year?

2. If not, is the Farmington Valley Montessori School appropriate?

3. If so, should the Student be placed at the Farmington V alley Montessori School?

4. Is the Student entitled to reimbursement of expenses incurred for 201 1, 2012 and/or 2013 Extended

School Year services?
5. Is the Student entitled to compensatory education relating to any period after July 22, 201 1?

PRO CEDURAL HISTORY:

The Student requested an impaltial special education hearing in the above-captioned matter on July 22,
2013. The Impaltial Hearing Ofﬁcer was appointed on July 26, 2013. A telephonic pre-hearing
conference was held 011 August 23, 2013. Attorney Jennifer Laviano appeared on behalf of the Student
and Attorney Susan Freedman appeared on behalf of the Glastonbury Board of Education,

Evidentiary hearings were scheduled for October 9, 2013 and October 22, 2013. The hearing scheduled
for October 9, 2013 was subsequently cancelled. On October 17, 2013, Counsel for the Student reported
that the parties had reached an agreement for resolution of the matter in principal and requested
additional time to ﬁnalize that agreement. The request was granted and the October 22, 2013 hearing
was cancelled. On November 15, 2013, Counsel for the Student reported to the Impaitial Hearing
Ofﬁcer that the matter was settled and requested that the matter be dismissed.

F INAL DECISION AND ORDER:

It is ordered that the Student’s request for dismissal is granted and this matter is dismissed.
