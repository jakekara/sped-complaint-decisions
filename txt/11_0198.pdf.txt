FINAL DECISION AND ORDER

ISSUES:

Whether the Student should continue receiving transition services through Project
Genesis?

SUMMARY:

On November 9, 2010, the Board received the Request for Due Process. A Prehearing
Conference was held on November 30, 2010 and hearing dates were scheduled. On December
22, 2010, the parties requested that the Hearing Ofﬁcer extend the deadline for mailing the Final
Decision and Order. That request was granted. On January 5, 201 1, Counsel for the Student
notiﬁed the Hearing Ofﬁcer that the parties had reached a settlement agreement and withdrew the
Due Process Request without prejudice.

FINAL DECISION AND ORDER:

The matter is DISMISSED, without prejudice.
