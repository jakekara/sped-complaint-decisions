FINAL DECISION AND ORDER
ISSUES:

1. Whether the Board failed to offer the Student FAPE for the 2009-10, 2010-1 1 and 201 1—12
school years;

2. If not, whether the Board shall pay for the residential program at Vista, as well as the Vista
evaluation;

3. Whether the Student is entitled to compensatory education.

PROCEDURAL HISTORY/DISCUSSION:

This request for hearing was received by the Board on July 18, 201 1 and a prehearing conference
convened on July 28, 201 1. A mediation session was convened on August 16, 201 1 which did not
result in an agreement at that time.

The hearing convened on September 13, 201 1 with the Parents’ attorney, the Parents, the Board Director
and the Board’s attorney present. At the hearing, the parties ﬁnalized an agreement, and reported that

the case was settled. The Parents’ attorney requested that the case be withdrawn with prejudice.
Therefore, this case is dismissed.

FINAL DECISION AND ORDER:

The matter is DISMISSED, with prejudice.
