  FINAL DECISION AND ORDER  The prehearing conference in this matter was held on December 22, 2004.  The Board 
was not available to participate.  A hearing date was set and both parties expressed an interest in mediating but were not able to 
communicate to set a date.  The date for mailing 
of the final decision was January 31, 2005.  At the hearing on January 24, 2005 the 
parties requested and were granted time to try and resolve the matter.  They reported back 
that they were able to reach agreement and that the matter could be dismissed.  
  FINAL DECISION AND ORDER:  The hearing is dismissed.
