  FINAL DECISION AND ORDER  ISSUES:  1. Are the Individualized Education Program (IEP) and placement offered by the Board 
of Education appropriate to Student&#8482;s special education needs in the least restrictive environment? 
 2. If not, is placement at Eagle Hill in Greenwich appropriate? 
 3. If Eagle Hill is an appropriate placement, is the Board responsible for funding that 
placement?
