FINAL DECISION AND ORDER
ISSUES:
1. Did the Board of Education offer the Student a free appropriate public education from
October 2012 to the present?

2. If not, is Winston Preparatory School an appropriate program for the Student?

If so, is the Student entitled to reimbursement for the expenses of the Winston

Preparatory School program?

4. Did the Board of Education develop the Student’s individualized education program in
accordance with the procedural and substantive requirements of the Individuals with
Disabilities Education Improvement Act?

5. If not, is the Student entitled to compensatory education?

W

PROCEDURAL HISTORY:

The Student submitted a Request for Impanial Special {Education Hearing, which was received
by the Board of Education on April 2, 2013. The Hearing Ofﬁcer was assigned to the case on
April 5, 2013. A telephonic prehearing conference was conducted on April 19, 2013. Counsel
for each party participated in the prehearing conference. Hearing dates of May 30, 2013 and

June 5, 2013 were scheduled.

On May 17, 2013, Counsel for the Student notiﬁed the Hearing Ofﬁcer that the Student
requested to withdraw her Request for Due Process Hearing without prejudice.

FINAL DECISION AND ORDER:

Accordingly, it is hereby ordered that this case shall be dismissed without prejudice.
