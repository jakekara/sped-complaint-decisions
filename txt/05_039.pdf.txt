     FINAL DECISION AND ORDER
   The prehearing conference in this matter was conducted on February 22, 2005.  Although 

there was some confusion initially the parties ultimately agreed to hearing dates and 
during the course of the hearing, to mediation.  The date for mailing of the final decision 
was set at March 25, 2005 and was extended to May 28, 2005 by agreement of the 
parties.  There were a number of issues in this matter including stay put.  On the fourth 
day of hearing the parties reported that they were able to resolve the matter and requested 

that it be dismissed with prejudice.  
   FINAL DECISION AND ORDER  The matter is 
DISMISSED WITH PREJUDICE.
