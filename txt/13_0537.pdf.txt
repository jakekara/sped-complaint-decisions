FINAL DECISION AND ORDER

ISSUES:

1. Does the Board’s current program and placement for Student provide Student with a
Free and Appropriate Education in the Least Restrictive Environment?

2. If the Board’s current program and placement for Student are not appropriate, is a
placement outside of the district appropriate?

PROCEDURAL HISTORY AND SUMMARY:

On June 7, 2013, the Hartford Board of Education received a request for a special education due
process hearing and the undersigned hearing ofﬁcer was appointed. On July 3, 2013, a

preheating conference was held and hearing dates of August 1, 2013, August 5, 2013 and August

20, 2013 were set. The deadline for mailing the ﬁnal decision and order was established as
August 21, 2013. On July 24, 2013, Attorney for the Student requested that the ﬁrst two

scheduled hearing dates be postponed, so that the parties could engage in settlement negotiations.

An extension of the mailing deadline was requested by the Attorney for the Board on J uly 25,
2013. Both requests were granted. The revised deadline for mailing the ﬁnal decision and order
was established as September 20, 20131 On August 14, 2013, Counsel for the Parents withdrew

the hearing request.

FINAL DECISION AND ORDER:

In light of the above facts, the case is dismissed.
