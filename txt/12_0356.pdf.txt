FINAL DECISION AND ORDER

ISSUES:
1. Did the BOE offer Student an appropriate program?

2. Should the BOE be required to ﬁnancially support an appropriate out of district
placement for Student, including transportation costs?

SUMMARY:

Student’s Guardian Ad Litem requested the special education Due Process hearing by letter
dated March 19, 2012 and received by the Board of Education (“BOB”) on March 28, 2012.
The deadline for mailing of the ﬁnal decision is June 4, 2012. The Prehearing Conference
was held on April 12, 2012. At the Prehearing Conference, the Guardian Ad Litem informed
the Hearing Ofﬁcer and the BOE that he might be withdrawing the Request for Due Process.
On April 17, 2012, the Guardian Ad Litem requested a withdrawal for the Due Process
hearing without prejudice. Accordingly, the case is DISMISSED without prejudice.

FINAL DECISION AND ORDER:

The matter is DISMISSED without prejudice.
