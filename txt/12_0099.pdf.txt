FINAL DECISION AND ORDER
ISSUES:

1. Whether the Board has offered the Student an appropriate program for the 201 1-12 school year;

2. Whether the Board has committed procedural violations during the 2011-12 school year which
have resulted in a denial of FAPE to the Student;

Whether the Student is entitled to compensatory education for the denial of FAPE during the
2011—12 school year;

b.)

4. Whether the Student shall be provided two hours of OT per week as well as an independent FBA
for the 201 1—12 school year.

PROCEDURAL HISTORY/DISCUSSION:

This request for hearing was received by the Board on October 12, 2011 and a prehearing conference
convened on October 19, 201 1. The patties agreed to mediate the case, which was convened on
November 21, 2011.

The initial hearing date of December 7, 201 1 was postponed and the mailing date of the decision was
extended at the request of the Parents’ attorney to allow the patties to attempt to ﬁnalize a settlement
agreement. On January 3, 2012 prior to the rescheduled hearing date of January 12, 2012 the Parents’
attorney submitted notiﬁcation that the settlement was ﬁnalized, and requested to withdraw the case
with prejudice. Therefore, this matter is dismissed.

FINAL DECISION AND ORDER:

The matter is DISMISSED, with prejudice.
