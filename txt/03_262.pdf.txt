   FINAL DECISION AND ORDER  ISSUES:    1. Whether the Board failed to provide an appropriate program for the 2001-2002 
school year.  2. Whether the Board failed to offer an appropriate program for the 2002-2003 
school year including extended school year services.  3. Whether the Board failed to convene a PPT to plan for the 2002-2003 school year. 
 4. Whether the Board failed to offer an appropriate program and placement for the 
2003-2004 school year.  5. Whether the Board failed to properly evaluate the Student. 
 6. Whether the Board should reimburse the parents for their privately obtained 
evaluations conducted by Dr. Michael Powers.  7. Whether the Board should reimburse the parents for their privately provided ABA 
program, including related services of speech and language and occupational 
therapy.  8. Whether the Student is entitled to compensatory education.
