FINAL DECISION AND ORDER
ISSUES:
1. Should the Board of Education have conducted an evaluation to determine the

Student’s eligibility for special education and related services?
2. If so, what is an appropriate remedy?

PROCEDURAL HISTORY:

The Student requested an expedited special education due process hearing in the above-
captioned matter on March 5, 2014. This Impartial Hearing Ofﬁcer was also appointed on
March 5, 2014,

A telephonic pre—hearing conference was held on March 1 1, 2014. Attorney Alexis Smith
appeared on behalf of the Student and Attorney Michelle Laubin appeared on behalf of the
Board of Education. A hearing was scheduled for March 31, 2014.

On March 19, 2014 Counsel for the Student reported that the controversy had been resolved
between the parties and requested that the Impartial Hearing Ofﬁcer dismiss the matter.
FINAL DECISION AND ORDER:

It is ordered that the Student’s request for dismissal is granted and this matter is dismissed
with prejudice.
