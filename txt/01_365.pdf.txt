Mangs   PROCEDURAL HISTORY:     This hearing was requested by the parents on November 29, 2001.  A prehearing conference was convened on December 5, 2001 during which hearing dates were set for January 10 and 23, 2002.  The hearing convened on January 10, 2002.  At the beginning of the hearing, the parties informed the hearing officer that an agreement had been reached and requested that they be given time to finalize their agreement.  The hearing officer stated that the hearing would be held open until January 23, 2002 at which time the hearing would be dismissed if no further communication was received.  As of January 23, 2002, the parties did not communicate with the hearing officer in any way.  FINAL DECISION AND ORDER:  This hearing is dismissed.
