FINAL DECISION AND ORDER

ISSUE:

Are the Board’s most recent speech and language and psycho-educational evaluations
sufﬁciently appropriate to deny the Parent’s request for an individual educational
evaluation at public expense?

PROCEDURAL HISTORY:

This hearing was requested by the Board on October 31, 2012. The Hearing Ofﬁcer was
appointed on that same date. The date for mailing the ﬁnal decision and order was
January 14, 2013.

The prehearing conference was held on November 21, 2012. The above issue is the sole
issue in this matter. The Parties set a Due Process Hearing date for December 17, 2012,
and agreed to pursue mediation prior to that date in an attempt to resolve the issue.
Mediation was scheduled and held on December 6, 2012. The Parties resolved the issue
through mediation, and on December 7, 2012 the Board withdrew their complaint and
request for a Due Process hearing.

FINAL DECISION AND ORDER:

Pursuant to the written request of the Board, through counsel, that the matter had been
1'eso1ved and that they wished to withdraw their complaint, and with no further issues to
be addressed, the MATTER IS DISMISSED.
