Hearing Officer  FINAL DECISION AND ORDER ISSUES:  1.  Whether the Board&#8482;s program for the 2004-2005 school year is appropriate. 
 2.  Whether the Student requires extended day services. 
 3.  Whether the Student requires a residential program to receive an appropriate 
education.  4.  Whether the program at Evergreen Center is appropriate for the Student. 
 5.  Whether the Student is entitled to compensatory education. 
 SUMMARY:  This matter was assigned on December 14, 2005, and a prehearing conference 
was held on December 17.  An extension of the mailing date of the decision was granted 
in accordance with Section 10-76h-9(e) of the Connecticut Regulations which provides for a 30 day postponement for settlement discussions between the parties.  On the first 

day of hearing on January 31, the parties agreed to a settlement of this case.  As the 
settlement agreement  required a substantial amount of time to effectuate, both parties 
requested that this matter be dismissed, 
without
 prejudice.    FINAL DECISION AND ORDER:  The matter is 
DISMISSED, without prejudice.
