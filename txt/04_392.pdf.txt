Hearing Officer   FINAL DECISION AND ORDER  The Board was the moving party in this matter and the prehearing conference was held 
on December 23, 2004. The parent was not available to participate. The date for mailing 
of the final decision was January 28, 2005.  The first hearing date was set for January 14, 2005 and both parties appeared.  The parties requested time to try to resolve the matter 
and this was granted.  They reported back that the matter had been resolved to their 
mutual satisfaction and agreed that the matter should be dismissed and future hearing 

dates cancelled.    FINAL DECISION AND ORDER:  The hearing is dismissed.
