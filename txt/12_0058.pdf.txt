FINAL DECISION AND ORDER
ISSUES:

1. Whether the Board failed to convene a PPT to hold a manifestation determination in
violation of20 U.S.C. §1415(k);

2. Whether the expulsion hearing shall be postponed or, if the Student is expelled,
whether the Parents’ proposed alternative remedy shall be considered.

PROCEDURAL HISTORY/DISCUSSION:

This request for an expedited hearing was submitted by the Parents on August 3, 201 l and a
prehearing conference convened on August 15, 201 1. The Board attorney was not present at the
prehearing conference, although the prehearing conference was noticed and included the
instructions that “[i]f either party has a conﬂict, please consult with the other party for a
mutually acceptable alternative time and date. The Board attorney’s legal assistance indicated
by email that the Board attorney was not available for the preheating conference, but did not
report an agreement on an agreed upon date/time for the prehearing conference

Prior to the hearing date, the Parents” attorney submitted notice that the manifestation
determination PPT meeting was convened and that the Parents withdraw their request for due
process. Therefore, this matter is dismissed.

FINAL DECISION AND ORDER:

The matter is DISMISSED.
