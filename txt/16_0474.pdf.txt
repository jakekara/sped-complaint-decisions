FINAL DECISION AND ORDER

A special education hearing in the above-captioned matter was requested by Student’s Parents
via a Request for Impartial Special Education Hearing dated April 18, 2016.2 A pre-hearing
conference was scheduled for May 9. The Board of Education (“BOB”) was not available on that
date and indicated that they believed that the matter was resolved. On May 12, Student’s Parents
requested in writing that the case be dismissed with prejudice.

ISSUES:
The Request for Hearing identiﬁes several issues under the following broader issues:
1. Did the Board of Education provide Student with a free appropriate public
education?
2. If the answer to Issue One is in the negative, what shall be the remedy?

F INAL DECISION AND ORDER

In light of the above facts, the above-captioned case is dismissed with prejudice
