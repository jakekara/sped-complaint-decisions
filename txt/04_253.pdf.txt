  FINAL DECISION AND ORDER  This matter had been filed by the parent, through counsel, on July 28, 2004.  A 
prehearing conference was conducted and the parties agreed to hearing dates and an extension of the forty-five days to mail the final decision.  The new date for mailing of 
the final decision was extended to November 14, 2004. The parties then requested a 
cancellation of the first date of hearing and that was granted.  Prior to the second date of hearing the parties advised that the matter had been resolved. 
  FINAL DECISION AND ORDER  The matter is 
DISMISSED.
