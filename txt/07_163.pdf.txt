  FINAL DECISION AND ORDER 
   SUMMARY:  The request for hearing filed by the 
Parent was received by the Board, and 
received at the state Department of Education on June 13.

, an unsigned notice that the Parent wanted 
an opportunity to consult with an attorney be
fore proceeding further,
 and did not want to 
proceed with the hearing at this time.   
 FINAL DECISION AND ORDER:
  The matter is 
DISMISSED, without prejudice.
