     FINAL DECISION AND ORDER
  ISSUE  Whether a residential placement for the student is necessary and appropriate. 
  SUMMARY  A prehearing conference was conducted in this matter on September 25, 2003.  The 

parties requested time to participate in mediation and this was granted.  The mediation 
was not successful.  This matter was scheduled for the hearing to begin on November 14, 
2003.  Notice of the hearing date was faxed to both parties on October 17, 2003.  Upon arriving for the hearing, a reception secretary informed the hearing officer that the 
hearing was cancelled.  Neither attorney appeared.  The hearing officer went on the 
record and dismissed the matter. 
  FINAL DECISION AND ORDER  The matter is 
DISMISSED without prejudice.
