FINAL DECISION AND ORDER
ISSUES:

1. Did the Bristol Board of Education offer the Student a free appropriate public
education for the 2012/2013 and 2013/2014 School Years?

2. If not, is the Focus Center appropriate for the Student?
3. If so, is the Student entitled to reimbursement for the expenses of the Focus Center

placement and/or placement at the F ocus Center?

PROCEDURAL HISTORY:

The Student ﬁled this due process case on June 19, 2013. This Impartial Hearing Ofﬁcer
was appointed to the matter on June 18, 2013. The deadline for submitting the ﬁnal
decision was established to be August 30, 2013. A prehearing conference was held on
June 28, 2013. The Student’s mother appeared on behalf of the Student and Kim Hapken
appeared on behalf of the Board of Education. At that time, hearings were scheduled for
August 22, 2013 and August 23, 2013.

On July 12, 2013, the Student’s mother advised this Impartial Hearing Ofﬁcer that the
matter was resolved and requested that it be dismissed.

FINAL DECISION AND ORDER:

It is ordered that the Student’s request for dismissal is granted, and this case is dismissed.
