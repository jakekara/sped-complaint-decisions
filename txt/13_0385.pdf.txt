FINAL DECISION AND ORDER

ISSUE:

 

Was the evaluation conducted by the Board appropriate?

SUMMARY:

On April 1, 2013, the Trumbull Board of Education ﬁled a request for a special education due
process hearing. On April 8, 2013, a prehearing conference was held and hearing dates were set
for May 30, 2013 and June 13, 2013. During the preheating conference the Attorney for the
Student requested an extension of the deadline for the ﬁling of the ﬁnal decision and order in to
accommodate the scheduling of multiple hearing dates. This request was granted and the original
deadline for the mailing of the ﬁnal decision and order of May 16, 2013 was extended to June
14, 2013. On May 23, 2013, Attorney for the Board withdrew the Board’s hearing request.

FINAL DECISION AND ORDER:

In light of the above facts, the case is dismissed.
