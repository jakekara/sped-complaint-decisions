Hearing Officer      FINAL DECISION AND ORDER
  ISSUE:  1. Whether the Board failed to properly place and program for the Student. 
 SUMMARY:  A prehearing conference was held in this matter on September 30, 2002 at which time it 
was agreed that the date for mailing of the final decision would be extended by thirty 
days for the purposes of attempting to resolve the matter.  The date for mailing of the 
final decision was thus extended to December 8, 2002.  Upon arriving at the hearing the 
hearing officer was informed that the parties had agreed to the following: 
 1. The Board would reimburse the Mother for fees paid to Marshall Gladstone, PhD 
for an evaluation.  2. The parties agreed Dr. James Black would evaluate the Student at Board expense 
and the evaluation will be made available to the parties before the next scheduled 

PPT meeting. 
 3. An occupational therapy evaluation of the Student will be scheduled by the Board 
and will be conducted by the Board staff.
