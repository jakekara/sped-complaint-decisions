PROCEDURAL HISTORY:

on July 22, 2015. The Impartial Hearing Ofﬁcer was appointed on July 23, 2015. A
telephonic pre—hearing conference was scheduled for August 7, 2015, The Student’s mother

appeared on behalf of the Student and Attorney Gwen Zittoun appeared on behalf of the
Board of Education.

On August 6, 2015, the Student reported that the parties had ﬁnally resolved their dispute, and
requested that the case be dismissed.

FINAL DECISION AND ORDER:

It is ordered that the Student’s request for dismissal is granted and this matter is dismissed.
