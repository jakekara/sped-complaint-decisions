The Student is 5 years old, has been as identiﬁed as Intellectualiy Disabled and is entitled to
receive a free and appropriate public education (“FAPE”) as deﬁned in the Individuals with
Disabilities Education Improvement Act (IDEIA) 20 U.S.C. §1401 et seq. and Connecticut
General Statute §10-76a. At a PPT meeting, the Parent rejected the program offered by the Board
for the 2010-20] 1 school year. The Parent requested a specialized program, evaluations and an
