Hearing Officer   FINAL DECISION AND ORDER  The prehearing conference in this matter was held on May 25, 2004.  Both parties 
participated and at that time they advised that they had reached a settlement that was in 
draft form.  They agreed that the Parent would forward the hearing officer a copy of the 
settlement agreement once it was finalized and that, after receiving that agreement, the 
hearing officer would dismiss the matter without prejudice.  The Settlement Agreement 
was received on June 4, 2004.   FINAL DECISION AND ORDER:  The hearing is dismissed without prejudice.
