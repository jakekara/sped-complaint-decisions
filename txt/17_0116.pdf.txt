FINAL DECISION AND ORDER

ISSUES:

1. Did the Board of Education offer the Student a Free Appropriate Public Education for the
2014/2015, 2015/2016 and 2016/2017 School Years, including the 2015 Extended School Year
and the 2016 Extended School Year?
a. If the Board of Education did not so provide, does the Student require a residential
placement?
b. If the Board of Education did not so provide and the Student requires a residential
placement, is the Grove School residential program appropriate?
c. If the Board of Education did not so provide and the Student requires a residential
placement and the Grove School is appropriate, should the Student be reimbursed for
the expense of the Grove School residential program and/or be placed at the Grove
School residential program?

2. 1f the Board of Education did not provide a free appropriate public education for the 2015
Extended School Year and the Student required a residential program at that time, was the
Trails program appropriate?

a. If so, should the Student be reimbursed for the expense of the Trails program?

3. If the Board of Education did not provide a free appropriate public education for any part of
the relevant time, is the Student entitled to compensatory education?
