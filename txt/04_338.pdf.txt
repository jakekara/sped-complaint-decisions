  FINAL DECISION AND ORDER   The prehearing conference in this matter was conducted on October 26, 2004.  The 
original date for mailing of the final decision was December 3, 2004.  Hearing dates were 
set and by agreement of the parties. The date for mailing of the final decision was 
extended.  The parties requested another continuance which was granted on the condition that the matter would be dismissed without prejudice if the matter did not go forward on 
January 11, 2005 as had been scheduled.  The date for mailing of the final decision was 

again extended.  On January 5, 2005 the Parent notified the hearing officer that the matter 
was in the process of being settled and that the hearing would not be necessary.  
 FINAL DECISION AND ORDER:  The hearing is dismissed without prejudice.
