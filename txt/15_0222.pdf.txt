FINAL DECISION AND ORDER

ISSUE:

1. Did the New Britain Board of Education fail to comply with a mediated settlement
agreement between the parties?

PROCEDURAL HISTORY:

The Student requested a special education due process hearing on November 6, 2014. This
Impartial Hearing Ofﬁcer was assigned to the case on November 7, 2014. A prehearing
conference was conducted on November 20, 2014. The Student’s Parent appeared on behalf
of the Student and Attorney Julie Fay appeared on behalf of the New Britain Board of
Education.

At the conference, the Student stated that the dispute had been fully and ﬁnally resolved,
and that this case should be dismissed.

FINAL DECISION AND ORDER:

It is ordered that the Student’s request for dismissal is granted and this matter is dismissed.
