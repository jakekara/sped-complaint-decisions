 ISSUES:  1. Whether the IEP developed for the Student&#8482;s 2007-2008 
school year was sufficient to provide the Student 
a free and appropriate public education (&#64257;FAPE&#64258;). 
 a. If not, whether the Parents&#8482; unilateral placement of the Student at the Winston Preparatory School

thereby entitling the Parents to reimbursement. 
 2. Whether the IEP developed for the Student&#8482;s 2008-2009
