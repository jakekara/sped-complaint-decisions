       FINAL DECISION AND ORDER 
   The prehearing conference in this matter was scheduled for November 26, 2003.  By 

agreement of the parties, the hearing was set for January 2, 2004, cancelled and 

rescheduled for January 12, 2004, cancelled and rescheduled for January 16, 2004, 
cancelled and rescheduled for February 2, 2004.  At the hearing of February 2, 2004, 
attended only by the Board, it was reported that the parties were working toward 
settlement.  On February 19, 2004 the Student&#8482;s counsel reported that a settlement 

agreement had been executed and withdrew the matter. 
  FINAL DECISION AND ORDER  The matter is 
DISMISSED.
