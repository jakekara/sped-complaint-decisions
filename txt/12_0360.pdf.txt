FINAL DECISION AND ORDER

ISSUES:

1. Whether the Board’s proposed program for the Student for the 2012-13 school year is
appropriate;

2. If not, whether the Student shall be paced at Watkinson School or Bancroft School as
requested by the Parents.

PROCEDURAL HISTORY/DISCUSSION:

The request for hearing was received by the Board on April 2, 2012 and a prehearing conference
convened on April 13, 2012. At the preheating conference, the patties conﬁrmed that they
agreed to mediation, but did not have a scheduled mediation date at that time.

On May 18, 2012, prior to the ﬁrst hearing date, the Parents’ attorney submitted notiﬁcation that
the case was settled. Therefore, this matter is dismissed.

FINAL DECISION AND ORDER:

The matter is DISMISSED.
