FINAL DECISION AND ORDER

SUMMARY AND PROCEDURAL HISTORY:

The Student is entitled to receive FAPE as deﬁned in the Individuals with Disabilities Education
Act (IDEA) 20 U.S.C. §1401 et seq. and Connecticut General Statutes §10-76a et seq. At a
planning and placement team (PPT) meeting, the Parent expressed disagreement with a proposed
placement for the Student, On November 12, 2015, the Parent ﬁled a due process hearing
request. An impartial hearing ofﬁcer was appointed on November 16, 2015 and a pre-hearing
conference was held on November 23, 2015.

In response to the Parent’s hearing request, the Board ﬁled a Sufﬁciency Challenge. On
November 23, 2015, the undersigned hearing ofﬁcer determined that the Parent’s request for due
process did not meet the requirements for a request for hearing. The Board requested that the
parties be allowed to proceed to a resolution session before ﬁling a Motion to Dismiss; this
request was granted.

In an electronic transmission, dated December 14, 2015, the Parent informed the hearing ofﬁcer
that the parties had signed a resolution agreement. The date for the mailing the Final Decision
and Order is January 29, 2016.

FINAL DECISION AND ORDER:

THE MATTER IS DISMISSED.
