 PROCEDURAL HISTORY  
 The Parent (Father) filed a due process hearing request with the Ansonia Board of Education on October 27, 2006.  The Board sent a copy to the State Department of 
Education (SDE) on that date.  This Hearing Officer was assigned to the case on October 30.  On November 7 a notice of a prehearing conference scheduled for November 14 was 

sent to the Parent and the Board's attorney.  
On November 9 the Board's attorney sent the 
Hearing Officer a copy of the Parent&#8482;s letter to the Director of Special Education and "it's 
attorney's office (Patricia M. Strong)" indicati
ng that he was satisfied with the outcome at 
a PPT meeting the previous day and wished to withdraw his complaint.  The Hearing 

Officer wrote a letter to the parties on November 12 and advised them that the case would 
be dismissed.  The mailing date for the decision is January 11, 2007.  
 FINAL DECISION AND ORDER 
 It is ordered that this case be dismissed.
