FINAL DECISION AND ORDER

ISSUES:

Does the provision of a free and appropriate public education (“FAPB”) to the
Student require an extended school year (“ESY”) for the summer 0f2012—2013?

PROCEDURAL HISTORY:

This complaint was ﬁled on April 1, 2013 by the Surrogate Parent. The resolution
meeting was waived by agreement of the parties in favor of mediation. Mediation
occurred on May 3, 2013, with the result of resolving the issues. The Surrogate Parent
withdrew the request for a due process hearing, with prejudice, on May 7, 2013.

FINAL DECISION AND ORDER:
The Surrogate Parent withdrew, in writing, the request for a due process hearing, with

prejudice on May 7, 2013. Having no other issues in this matter to resolve, this matter is
Dismissed with prejudice.
