Appearing on behalf of the Board:    Attorney Marsha Belman Moses Berchem, Moses & Devlin  75 Broad Street Milford, CT  06460  Appearing before:      Attorney Mary Elizabeth Oppenheim        Hearing Officer   FINAL DECISION AND ORDER   This matter was assigned on September 23, 2002, and a notice of prehearing conference was sent via facsimile to the Board and via U.S mail to the Parent.1  The prehearing conference convened on September 30 as scheduled.
