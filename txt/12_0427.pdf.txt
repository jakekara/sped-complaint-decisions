FINAL DECISION AND ORDER

ISSUES

1. Are the provisions of the Student’s IEP for Occupational Therapy appropriate?
2. If not, should an additional hour of Occupational Therapy provided in an outside clinical

setting be added?

PRO CEDURAL HISTORY

The Student submitted a Request for Impartial Special Education Hearing on June 4, 2012. The
Hearing Ofﬁcer was assigned to the case on the same day, June 4, 2012. A telephonic pre-
hearing conference was held on June 12, 2012. Hearing dates of July 9, 2012 and July 12, 2012
were scheduled. At the subsequent request of the BOB, the hearing dates of July 9, 2012 and
July 12, 2012 were cancelled, and hearing dates of July 17, 2012 and July 20, 2012 were
scheduled instead.

On June 26, 2012, the Student requested that the hearings be cancelled and the matter be
withdrawn without prej udice.

FINAL DECISION AND ORDER

It is ordered that this case is dismissed without prejudice.
