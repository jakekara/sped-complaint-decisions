       FINAL DECISION AND ORDER    PROCEDURAL HISTORY and SUMMARY:  This hearing was requested on March 4, 2003.  The prehearing conference was held on March 11, 2002.  The hearing was convened on April 29, 2003 at which time the parties 
notified the hearing officer that they were close to reaching a settlement of the matter.  It 
was agreed that the parents would either withdraw their request for hearing or the hearing 

would be dismissed on May 21, 2003.  As of the close of business on May 21, 2003, 
neither party has communicated with the hearing officer. 
   FINAL DECISION AND ORDER:  This hearing is dismissed.
