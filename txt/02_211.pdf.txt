PROCEDURAL HISTORY:  This hearing was requested by the Parents on August 1, 2002, while an expulsion hearing for Student was pending.  The hearing officer was appointed on August 1, and a pre-hearing conference was held by conference telephone call on August 7.  Both parties agreed in the pre-hearing conference that the hearing should be expedited, pursuant to Section 10-76h-10, Regulations of Connecticut State Agencies (R.C.S.A.) and 34 C.F.R. Section 300.528.  The Board stayed the expulsion hearing for Student, pending the completion of this special education hearing.
