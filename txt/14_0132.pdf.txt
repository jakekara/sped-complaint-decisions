PROCEDURAL HISTORY/DISCUSSION:

This hearing request was received by the Board on August 21, 2013, and a preheating conference
convened on August 29, 2013. At the preheating conference the attorneys for both parties
conﬁrmed that the parties agreed to mediate this case

The Parents‘ request for an extension of the mailing date of the decision was granted so that the
parties had sufﬁcient time to settle this case, and a hearing date of October 28, 2013 was
scheduled. Prior to the ﬁrst hearing date, the Parents’ attorney submitted notiﬁcation that the
parties had resolved the case and that the Parents were withdrawing their request for due process
with prejudice. Therefore, this matter is dismissed.

FINAL DECISION AND ORDER:

The matter is DISMISSED.
