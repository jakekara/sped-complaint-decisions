October 9, 2002 -1- Final Decision and Order 02-301 .     STATE OF CONNECTICUT DEPARTMENT OF EDUCATION    Student v. Vocational-Technical School System  Appearing on behalf of the Parent:    The parent proceeded pro se  Appearing on behalf of the School System:  Attorney Beatrice Tinty  Vocational-Technical School System 25 Industrial Park Road Middletown, CT  06457  Appearing before:      Attorney Mary Elizabeth Oppenheim  Hearing Officer   FINAL DECISION AND ORDER   This matter was assigned on October 4, 2002, and a notice of prehearing conference was sent via facsimile to the Board and via U.S mail to the Parent. The prehearing conference convened on October 8 as scheduled.  The School System counsel was present and ready to proceed at the time of the prehearing, but the Parent was not present at her telephone number at the time of the prehearing.  At the time of the scheduled prehearing conference, a message was left at the Parent&#8482;s telephone number, which gave her an opportunity to request a second prehearing conference.  Neither the parent, nor the School System counsel contacted the undersigned hearing officer with a request for a second prehearing conference.    FINAL DECISION AND ORDER:  The matter is DISMISSED, without prejudice.
