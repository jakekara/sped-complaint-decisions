FINAL DECISION AND ORDER 
A special education hearing in the above-captione
d matter was requested by the Board of Education 
via Request for Impartial Special 
Education Hearing dated July 9, 2010.1 The request was received by Student on July 13. The thirty-day resolution period runs through August 12 and the deadline for mailing the 
final decision and order is September 26, 2010. 
A pre-hearing conference was held on August 2. Attorney Shaffer appeared on behalf of Student and 
Attorney Moses appeared on behalf of the Board of Education. The following issue was identified: Whether 
the Board of Education is required to provide an independent evaluation of Student. The parties participated in a resolution meeting on August 5. On August 11, the Board of Education 
indicated in writing that the parties had resolved the 
matter and that the Board of
 Education withdraws the request for due process. FINAL DECISION AND ORDER 
In light of the above facts, the above-captioned case is hereby dismissed.
