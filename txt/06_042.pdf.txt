Hearing Officer       FINAL DECISION AND ORDER  The prehearing in this matter was conducted on February 20, 2006.  Both parties 
participated.  The Parent indicated she was withdrawing her request for due process and would file again if the need arose.  She understood that there is a two year statute of limitations.  She indicated that the Board had agreed to conduct an evaluation.  The 
parties agreed that there would be no change in programming at this time but that a PPT 
would be convened to discuss the results of the evaluation once it is completed. 
 FINAL DECISION AND ORDER:  The hearing is dismissed without prejudice.
