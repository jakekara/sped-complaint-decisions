Hearing Officer   FINAL DECISION AND ORDER  SUMMARY:  This matter was filed on April 18, and prehearing conferences were held on April 
27 and April 28.  On April 28, the attorney for the Parent submitted a request for an 
extension of the mailing date of the decision so that the parties could attempt to settle the 
matter.  The Board&#8482;s attorney submitted exhibits regarding the Student&#8482;s placement and 

program. The request for extension was granted in accordance with Section 10-76h-9(e) 
of the Connecticut Regulations which provides for a 30-day postponement for settlement 
discussions between the parties.  On May 24, the attorney for the Parent submitted notice that the matter was 
settled.     FINAL DECISION AND ORDER:  The matter is 
DISMISSED.
