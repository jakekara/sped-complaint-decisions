FINAL DECISION AND ORDER

ISSUES

 

1. Did the Board of Education offer the Student a free appropriate public education
for the 2012~2013 School Year?

2, If not, what is the appropriate remedy?

PRO CEDURAL HISTORY

The Student submitted a Request for Impartial Special Education Hearing on August 29,
2012. The Hearing Ofﬁcer was assigned to the case on August 30, 2012. On September
9, 2012, the Hearing Ofﬁcer issued Notice of a telephonic pre~hearing conference to be
conducted on September 14, 2012.

On September 10, 2012, the Student informed the Hearing Ofﬁcer that the patties were
able to amicably resolve their dispute, and requested that the matter be dismissed without

prejudice.
FINAL DECISION AND ORDER

It is ordered that the Student's request for dismissal is granted, and this case is dismissed
without prejudice.
