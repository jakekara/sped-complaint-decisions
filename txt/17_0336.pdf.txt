FINAL DECISION AND ORDER
ISSUES:

1. Did the Individualized Education Plan “IEP” developed by the District and implemented
during the 2015-2016 school year fail to provide Student with a Free Appropriate Public
Education (“FAPE”) under the Individuals with Disabilities Education Act (“IDEA”)?

2. Does Student’s IEP for the 2016—2017 school year fail to provide Student With a FAPE
under the IDEA because it lacks a Behavior Intervention Plan, assignment of a 1:1
paraprofessional, and a formal opportunity for Student to interact with non—disabled peers in a
group setting in order to work on Student’s social skills?
