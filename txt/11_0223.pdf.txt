ISSUES:
1, Whether the Board’s proposed program offers FAPE t0 the Student;
2. Whether the Parent should provide the items in Attachment A of his Health Care Plan
prior to the Student’s entrance into school;
3. Whether the Parent should send the Student to school on the carry—all with the Student’s
two private duty nurses or provide transponation with the Parent’s own vehicle;
4. Whether the Parent should send the Student to the School for the 2010—201 1 school year.
SUMMARY:

The Board ﬁled this request for hearing which the Parent received on November 20, 2010. A
Pre-Hearing Conference was held on December 13, 2010. On December 23, 2010, the Board requested that the
Hearing Ofﬁcer extend the deadline for mailing the Final Decision and Order. That request was granted and the
deadline was extended to March 5, 1022. On January 13, 2011, Counsel for the Board withdrew the request for
hearing without prejudice.

FINAL DECISION AND ORDER:

The matter is DISMISSED, without prejudice.
