FINAL DECISION AND ORDER

ISSUES

1. Was the Board’s June 2012 determination that the Student is no longer eligible for special
education and related services correct?

2, If not, what relief is appropriate?

SUMMARY

A due process hearing in the above—captioned matter was requested by the Parents and received
by the Board on September 28, 2012. The undersigned hearing ofﬁcer was appointed on
October 1, 2012. On October 10, 2012, a prehearing conference was held by telephone. The
Parents twice requested that the original decision deadline be extended. Those requests were
granted, extending the decision deadline to F ebruary 13, 2013.

Hearings took place on January 15 and 17, 2013. At the January 17 hearing, the panics
announced that they had reached a settlement agreement. The Parents accordingly withdrew

their request for due process.

FINAL DECISION AND ORDER

The matter is DISMISSED with prejudice.
