PROCEDURAL HISTORY:

The Student ﬁled this special education due process case on July 31, 2014. This Impartial
Hearing Ofﬁcer was appointed to hear the case on July 31, 2014. A prehearing conference
was held on August 29, 2014. The Student’s parents appeared for the Student and Attorney
Abby R. Wadler appeared for the Board of Education. At that time, the deadline for
completing the hearing and ﬁling the ﬁnal decision was established to be October 14, 2014.
The deadline was later extended to November 13, 2014. Hearings were scheduled for
October 28, 2014 and October 29, 2014.

On September 17, 2014, the Student reported that the parties reached a mutual agreement
and requested that the matter be dismissed.

FINAL DECISION AND ORDER:

It is ordered that the Student’s request for dismissal is granted and this matter is dismissed.
