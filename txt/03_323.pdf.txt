Hearing Officer    FINAL DECISION AND ORDER  ISSUES  I. Whether the Local Educational Agency provided the child with a free and 
appropriate public education.    PROCEDURAL HISTORY  The parent requested a Due Process hearing on October 7, 2003.  On October 8, 2003, the parties agreed to hearing dates of December 3, 10 and 11, 2003.  Hearing convened on 
December 3 and 10, 2003.  The parties believed they would be able to reach an 
agreement and requested a postponement of the December 11, 2003 hearing date. They 
anticipated the hearing would be dismissed with prejudice.  To date the parties have not 
requested additional dates for hearing.     FINAL DECISION AND ORDER  The case is dismissed with prejudice.
