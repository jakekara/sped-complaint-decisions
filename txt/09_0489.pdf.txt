A special education hearing in the above-captione
d matter was requested by the Board of Education 
via letter dated July 23, 2009. It was 
received by the Student on that da
te. The 30-day resolution period ran 
through August 22, 2009. The deadline for mailing th
e final decision and order is October 6, 2009. A pre-hearing conference was held on August 19, 2009. Attorney Alyce Alfano appeared on behalf of the Student and Attorneys Andreana Bellach and Alexis Cole appeared on behalf of the Board. The following issues were identified: 
1.Whether the therapeutic out-of-district day 
program recommended by the Board provides the 
Student with a free appropriate public education in the least restrictive environment;

parties reached an agreement. The 
Board withdrew its request for due 
process without prejudice in anticipation of the agreement being signed.
