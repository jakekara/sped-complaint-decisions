FINAL DECISION AND ORDER

SUMMARY and PROCEDURAL HISTORY:

The Student has been identiﬁed as Other Health Impaired and is entitled to receive FAPE as
deﬁned in the Individuals with Disabilities Education Improvement Act (IDEA) 20 U.S.C, §1401
et seq. and Connecticut General Statute §10-7Ga. At a PPT meeting, the Parent refused to allow
the Board to perform evaluations of the Student,

An impartial hearing ofﬁcer was appointed on July 14, 201 1 and a pre—hearing conference was
held on July 26, 201 I. A hearing date of September 23, 201 i was chosen by the parties. In an
electronic transmission, the Board’s attorney advised the hearing ofﬁcer that the panties were able
to create an IEP for the Student at a PPT meeting and that the evaluations requested by the Board
were no longer required. The Board requested that the matter be withdrawn without prejudice.

The withdrawal without prejudice was granted.

The date for mailing the Final Decision and Order was extended at the request of the Board in
order to accommodate the hearing date. The Date for mailing the Final Decision and Order is

September 27, 20] 1.

FINAL DECISION AND ORDER:

THE MATTER IS DISMISSED \VITHOUT PREJUDICE.
