FINAL DECISION AND ORDER 
A special education hearing in the above-captione
d matter was requested by the Student via Request 
for Due Process dated October 19, 2009. It was received 
by the Board of Education on the same date. The 
30-day resolution period ran through November 18, 2009.  
The deadline for mailing the final decision and 
order is January 2, 2010. A pre-hearing conference was held on November 
5, 2009. Attorney Skelley appeared on behalf of the Student and Ms. Keith and Principal Joe Morris appeared on behalf of the Board of Education. The following issues were identified: 
1.Did the Board of Education appropriately determine Student to be ineligible for special 
education? 
2.Should the Board of Education provide Student with special education testing? 3.If the answer to issue number one is no, is the Student entitled to compensatory education? 
The case was scheduled for hearing on Decembe
r 7, 2009 and December 10, 2009. Via letter dated 
December 2, 2009, the Student indicated 
that the parties reached a settlement agreement and requested that 
the matter be withdrawn with prejudice. 
FINAL DECISION AND ORDER 
In light of the above facts, the above-captioned case is hereby dismissed with prejudice.
