Hearing Officer   FINAL DECISION AND ORDER    The prehearing conference in this matter was held on March 17, 2004. Both parties 
participated in the prehearing conference.  The date for mailing of the final decision was 
originally set for April 23, 2004.  The parties agreed to participate in mediation and the 
hearing was set for April 30, 2004 to allow time for the mediation to be held.  At hearing 
on April 30, 2004 the Board informed the hearing officer that the Parent had withdrawn 
her request for hearing the afternoon before, for reason that the mediation had not yet 

been conducted and would be rescheduled.   FINAL DECISION AND ORDER:  The hearing is dismissed.
