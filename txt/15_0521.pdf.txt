ISSUE:

Is the Board of Education entitled to share conﬁdential student information with the
neuropsychologist retained to evaluate the Student, including providing such information to
the neuropsychologist and receiving such information from the neuropsychologist?

PROCEDURAL HISTORY:

The Board of Education ﬁled this request for a special education due process hearing on May
22, 2015. The Hearing Ofﬁcer was assigned to the case on May 22, 2015, and a preheating
conference was conducted on June 11, 2015. At that time, the parties agreed that the initial
deadline for ﬁling the ﬁnal decision was July 6, 2015. A hearing was scheduled for July 13,
2015. On J une 26, 2015, the Board of Education requested a thiity—day postponement and
extension of the deadline to issue the ﬁnal decision. The Student did not object to that request
and it was granted.

On July 2, 2015, the Board of Education requested that the hearing scheduled for July 13,
2015 be cancelled. Again, absent objection from the Student, the request was granted.

On July 16, 2015, the Board of Education requested that the matter be dismissed.

FINAL DECISION AND ORDER:

It is ordered that the Board of Education’s request for dismissal is granted and this matter is
dismissed.
