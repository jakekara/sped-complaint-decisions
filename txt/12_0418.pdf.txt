FINAL DECISION AND ORDER

ISSUES:

and Placement Team was appropriate or would result in the denial of a Free Appropriate Public
Education to Student; and

Education Due Process Hearing; the undersigned hearing ofﬁcer was appointed on the same date.

preheating conference on May 22, 2012, Parent notiﬁed the hearing ofﬁcer that the Parent was
withdrawing the hearing request without prejudice.

FINAL DECISION AND ORDER:
