Hearing Officer   FINAL DECISION AND ORDER  This matter was filed as an expedited hearing during the pendency of a due process 
matter that was proceeding along a nonexpedited track.   At hearing the parent appeared 
with an assistor.  At hearing the parent agreed to a dismissal of this matter to allow the 
parties to pursue mediation.  It was explained to the parties that if the matter was refiled 
in the future the forty five days would run from the date of the new filing.  
 FINAL DECISION AND ORDER:  The hearing is dismissed without prejudice.
