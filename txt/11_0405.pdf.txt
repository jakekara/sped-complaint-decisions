SUMMARY and PROCEDURAL HISTORY:

The Student has been identiﬁed as Other Health Impaired and is entitled to receive a free and
appropriate public education (FAPE) as deﬁned in the Individuals with Disabilities Education
Improvement Act (IDEIA) 20 U.S.C. §1401 et seq. and Connecticut General Statute §10-76a. At a PPT
meeting, the Parent rejected the program offered by the Board for the 201 1-2012 school year. The
Parent requested that the Board provide support services at Trinity Nursery School. The Board refused
the Parent’s request.

On or about April 15, 201 1, the Board received notice of the Parent’s request for due process. An
impartial hearing ofﬁcer was appointed on April 15, 201 1 and a pre-hearing conference was held on
April 27, 2011. A hearing date of June 8, 201] was chosen by the patties.

On or about April 29, 2011 the panics attended a resolution meeting. In an electronic
transmission, the Parent advised the hearing ofﬁcer that the palties were able to resolve the matter at a
resolution meeting and requested that the matter be withdrawn. The request was granted. The date for
mailing the Final Decision and Order is June 29, 201 1.

.FINAL DECISION AND ORDER:

THE MATTER IS DISMISSED.
