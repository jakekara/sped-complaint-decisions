        FINAL DECISION AND ORDER  Summary/Conclusions of Fact:  This hearing was requested on November 14, 2003.  A prehearing conference was 
convened on December 5, 2003 at which time the parties requested a continuance in 
order to pursue settlement.  The continuance was granted; a hearing date was set for 
January 13, 2004 and the date for the mailing of the final decision and order was changed 

to January 30, 2004.  On December 31, 2003, the Parent requested a postponement of the 
January 13, 2003 hearing date based upon the rescheduling of mediation due to the 
mediator&#8482;s unavailability.  Another postponement was granted; the hearing was 

rescheduled for February 3, 2004 and the date for the mailing of the final decision and 
order was changed to February 17, 2004.  On January 13, 2004, the hearing officer received notification from the Bureau of Speci
al Education that mediation had been held 
and an agreement had been reached by the parties.  Later that day, the hearing officer 
received a fax from the Parent requesting that
 the hearing be dismissed without prejudice 
so that the parties could complete negotiations. 
 Final Decision and Order:  This hearing is dismissed without prejudice.
