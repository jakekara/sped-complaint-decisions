FINAL DECISION AND ORDER 
A special education hearing in the above-captione
d matter was requested by the Student via letter 
dated August 31, 2009. The 30-day resolution period ran through September 30, 2009 and the original deadline for mailing the final decision and order was November 14, 2009. 
A pre-hearing conference was scheduled for September 30, 2009. Via motion dated September 24, 
2009, the Student requested that the pre-hearing conf
erence be postponed and that the mailing date be 
extended 30 days to permit the parties to pursue mediation
. The request for a 30-day extension was granted 
after full consideration of the positi
ons of the parties and the deadline for mailing the final decision and order 
was extended until December 14, 2009. The September 30, 
2009 pre-hearing conference was postponed until 
November 2, 2009. 
Via motion dated October 14, 2009, the Student reque
sted that the November 2, 2009 pre-hearing conference be postponed and an extension granted through November 30, 2009, the da
te on which the parties were scheduled for mediation. After full consideration of
 the positions of the parties, the request was granted 
and the deadline for mailing the final decision wa
s extended until January 13, 2010. The November 2, 2009 
pre-hearing conference was postponed until December 2, 2009. 
Via letter dated December 1, 2009, the Student indicat
ed that the parties had reached a settlement 
agreement and that the Student withdrew the request for due process. 
FINAL DECISION AND ORDER 
In light of the above facts, the above-captioned case is hereby dismissed with prejudice.
