FINAL DECISION AND ORDER

ISSUES:

1. Whether the Board’s proposed program for the Student for the 201 1-12 school year is
appropriate

2. If not, whether the Parents’ proposed placement of the Student at Eagle Hill is
appropriate and shall be reimbursed.

PROCEDURAL HISTORY/DISCUSSION:

This request for hearing was received by the Board on July 25, the date of the preheating
conference. The Board had not received a copy of the request by the prehearing conference, so
the hearing ofﬁcer forwarded a copy to the Board on that date.

The hearing convened on September 22, with the Parents’ attorney, the Parent, the Board
representatives and the Board’s attorney present. At the hearing, the parties ﬁnalized an

agreement, and reported that the case was settled. The Parents’ attorney requested that the case
be withdrawn with prejudice. Therefore, this case is dismissed.

FINAL DECISION AND ORDER:

The matter is DISMISSED, with prejudice.
