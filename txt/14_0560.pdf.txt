FINAL DECISION AND ORDER
ISSUES:

1. Does the Board’s proposed program for the Student for the 2014-2015 school year
provide Student with a Free Appropriate Public Education (“FAPE”)?

2. If the Board’s proposed program for the Student for the 2014-2015 school year does not
provide Student with a FAPE, what is the appropriate program for the Student?

PROCEDURAL HISTORY AND SUMMARY:

On May 30, 2014, the New Britain Board of Education received a Request for a Special
Education Due Process Hearing and the undersigned hearing ofﬁcer was appointed. On June 20
2014, a prehearing conference was held and hearing dates of July 15, 2014 and July 23, 2014
were set. The mailing deadline for the F inal Decision and Order was established as August 13,
2014. On June 30, 2014, Counsel for the Student withdrew the hearing request.

FINAL DECISION AND ORDER:

In light of the above facts, the case is dismissed.
