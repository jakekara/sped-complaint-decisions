FINAL DECISION AND ORDER
ISSUES:

1. Did the Board of Education offer the Student an appropriate program since May 23, 2011?

2. If not, is the Greenbriar Academy an appropriate placement for the Student?

3. 1f the response to Question No. l is negative, were past unilateral placements at Renfrew Greenwich,
Renfrew Philadelphia, Mirasol Eating Disorder Recovery Center and/or Silver Hill appropriate?

4. Did the Board of Education properly and timely fulﬁll its Child Find obligations with respect to the
Student since May 23, 2011?

5. Is the Student entitled to compensatory education or any other remedy relating to the time since May

23, 2013?
PROCEDURAL HISTORY:

The Student requested a special education hearing in the above—captioned matter on May 23, 2013. This
Hearing Ofﬁcer was appointed on May 29, 2013. A telephonic pre-hearing conference was held on June
10, 2013. The deadline for submitting the decision was established to be August 6, 2013, and hearings
were scheduled for July 9, 2013 and July 24, 2013.

On July 2, 2013, Counsel for the Student reported that the palties were pursuing settlement and
requested that the hearings be postponed and the deadline for submitting the decision in this matter
extended. These requests were granted.

On August 23, 2013, Counsel for the Student reported that the parties reached a settlement in principal
and required time to document their agreement. On September 12, 2013, Counsel for the Student
reported that the parties ﬁnalized their settlement agreement and requested that the matter be dismissed
with prejudice.

FINAL DECISION AND ORDER:

The Student’s request for dismissal is granted and this matter is hereby dismissed with prejudice.
