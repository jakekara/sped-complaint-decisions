F INAL DECISION AND ORDER

ISSUES:

1. Did the Board fail to provide Student 21 Free Appropriate Public Education in the Least
Restrictive Environment for the 2012—2013 academic year?

2. Did the Board fail to provide Student a Free Appropriate Public Education in the Least
Restrictive Environment by not providing Extended School Year Services?

3. Does the program offered to Student for the 2013—2014 academic year provide Student with
a Free Appropriate Public Education in the Least Restrictive Environment?

4. If the Board’s program for the 2013-2014 school year is inappropriate, is an out-of—district
placement at the Intensive Education Academy appropriate placement for Student?

PROCEDURAL HISTORY AND SUMMARY:

A Request for a Special Education Due Process Hearing was received by the Board on July 12,
2013. A prehearing conference was held on July 26, 2013 and the deadline for mailing the ﬁnal
decision and order was established as September 25, 2013 and three hearing dates in September
were set. On September 19, 2013 Attorney for the Parent requested an extension of the deadline for
the mailing of the ﬁnal order and decision and a postponement of the ﬁrst two of the hearing dates
in September in order to ﬁnalize an agreement. These requests were granted and a new deadline of
October 25 , 2013 was established A request for postponement of the third hearing date was made
and granted on September 16, 2013 and new hearing dates of October 15, 2013 and October 16,
2013 were set On October 11, 2013, the Attorney for the Parent withdrew the hearing request.

FINAL DECISION AND ORDER:

In light of the above facts, the case is dismissed.
