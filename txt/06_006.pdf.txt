Hearing Officer   FINAL DECISION AND ORDER   PROCEDURAL HISTORY   The Parent (Father) filed a due process hearing request on January 9, 2006.  This Hearing Officer was assigned to the case on January 10.  On January 26, the Hearing Officer was 
advised that Atty. Feinstein was representing the Parent.  A prehearing conference was held on January 31 with attorneys representing both parties.  A resolution meeting had 

been scheduled, but did not occur.  The parties were willing to mediate.  A hearing date 

was agreed on for March 3.  The decision deadline was set at March 27, 2006.  On 
February 17 the State Department of Education advised the Hearing Officer that the 

parties had reached an agreement through mediation on February 17 and that the hearing 

request was withdrawn.  The hearing for March 3 was canceled.  FINAL DECISION AND ORDER  It is ordered that this case be dismissed.
