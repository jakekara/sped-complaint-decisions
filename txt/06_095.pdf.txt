 PROCEDURAL HISTORY 
 The parent through counsel requested a due process hearing.  After attempts to settle the 
matter, the parties proceeded to hearing.  
On the second day of hearing the parties requested leave to pursue settlement discussi
ons once again.  The date for final decision was extended on the record to September 15, 2006, to accommodate additional dates for 
hearing.   On August 15, 2006 the parties reported they reached final settlement, the 
record closed on August 15, 2006.  The parties request the dismissal decision be reissued 
to reflect their agreement that the matter be 
dismissed without prejudice.  The matter was 
resolved in less than 45 days plus the extensions.    FINAL DECISION AND ORDER 
 1. The case is dismissed without prejudice.
