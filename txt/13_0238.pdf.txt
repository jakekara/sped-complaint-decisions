FINAL DECISION AND ORDER

ISSUES:

Speciﬁc issues were not agreed to by the Palties, in relation to the speciﬁc complaint ﬁled, as the
matter was resolved with a written agreement prior to the prehearing conference being held.

PROCEDURAL HISTORY:

This hearing was requested by Parent on November 26, 2012. The Hearing Ofﬁcer was
appointed on that same date. The date for mailing the decision was February 8, 2013.

The Pre—hearing conference was scheduled for December 6, 2012, at 1:00 PM. The Board
notified the Hearing Ofﬁcer by email on the evening of December 5, 2012 that the Patties had
reached agreement and that the prehearing conference was no longer required. On the morning
of December 6, 2012, the Parents notiﬁed the Hearing Ofﬁcer by email that an agreement had
been reached, that the prehearing conference was no longer required and that the Parents were
withdrawing their complaint. The email was attached with a formal letter from counsel

withdrawing the complaint.

FINAL DECISION AND ORDER:

Pursuant to the written request of the Parents, through counsel, that the matter had been
resolved and that they wished to withdraw their complaint, and with no further issues to be
addressed, the MATTER IS DISMISSED.
