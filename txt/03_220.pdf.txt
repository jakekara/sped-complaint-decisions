Hearing Officer   FINAL DECISION AND ORDER  ISSUES  I. Whether the Local Educational Agency provided the child with a free and 
appropriate public education.   PROCEDURAL HISTORY  The parent requested a Due Process hearing on July 10, 2003.  The parties agreed hearings would convene on September 9, 16, 29 30, October 15, 18, November 18, 19 
and December 2, 2004.   The appropriate request to extend the date for final decision was 
requested and granted on the record.  On November 18, 2003 the parties believed they 
would be able to reach an agreement and requested the hearings scheduled for November 
19 and December 2, 2003 be postponed until they had an opportunity to finalize a 

settlement.  There has been no request for additional hearing dates since November 18, 
2003.  FINAL DECISION AND ORDER  The case is dismissed with prejudice.
