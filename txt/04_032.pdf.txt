   FINAL DECISION AND ORDER    SUMMARY AND PROCEDURAL HISTORY:  The student is a five year old young child who has been classified as Learning Disabled and therefore eligible for special education and related services as defined in 20 USC &#167;&#167;1401 et seq. The parents requested a hearing on or about February 5, 2004.  A pre-hearing telephonic conference was scheduled for February 13, 2004. At the pre-hearing conference the parents through their attorney informed the hearing officer that they were 

in the process of resolving the matter and would forward a letter withdrawing the matter. 

On or about February 19, 2004 the parents&#8482; attorney sent a letter requesting that the matter be withdrawn without prejudice. 
   FINAL DECISION AND ORDER:  The matter is DISMISSED, WITHOUT PREJUDICE.
