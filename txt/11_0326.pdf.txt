FINAL DECISION AND ORDER

A special education hearing in the above-captioned matter was requested by Student’s
Mother Via Request for Impaltial Special Education Hearing dated February 18, 201 1.1 It was
received by the Board of Education (“BOB”) on February 22. The thirty-day resolution period
ran through March 22 and the deadline for mailing the ﬁnal decision and order was May 6.

The request for due process identiﬁed the following issue: Is BOE required to revise
Student’s BIP, complete an FBA, and complete an assistive technology evaluation?

A telephonic pre-hearing conference was scheduled for March 23. Via letter dated March
15, Student's Attorney withdrew the request for hearing without prejudice absent BOE’s

objection.
FINAL DECISION AND ORDER
In light of the above facts, the above—captioned case is hereby dismissed without
