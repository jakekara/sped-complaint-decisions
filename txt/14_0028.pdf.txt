FINAL DECISION AND ORDER

ISSUE:

Does the Student require a full residential placement at the proposed placement
site in order to receive a Free and Appropriate Public Education (“FAPE”) for the

2013 ~2014 academic year?

PROCEDURAL HISTORY:

The complaint was ﬁled on behalf of the Student on July 10, 20131 A pre-hearing
conference was held on July 23, 2013, in which the above issue was identiﬁed for
hearing. An initial hearing date was scheduled for September 20, 2013, On August 13,
2013 the Paﬂies requested a postponement of the due process hearing date and an
extension of the ﬁnal decision and order date. The request was granted and the due
process date was moved to October 7, 2013 with the ﬁnal decision and order date moved
to October 23, 2013. On September 20, 2013, the Patties engaged in mediation and
reached agreement. Subsequently the Parents requested to withdraw their complaint,

with prejudice.
FINAL DECISION AND ORDER:

The Parents have withdrawn their complaint, with prejudice, following their mediation
and successful resolution of this matter. W ith n0 ﬁnther issues to be resolved, this

matter is DISMISSED, with prejudice.
