PROCEDURAL HISTORY/DISCUSSION:

This request for hearing was received by the Board on August 5, 201 1 and a prehearing
conference convened on August 16, 201 1. At the prehearing conference, counsel for both
parties indicated that they had agreed to mediate the case in lieu of a resolution meeting.

On September 12, 201 1 the Parents” attorney reponed that the parties had worked out a
resolution in this matter and the agreement is in the process of being executed. The Parents’

attorney indicated that the Parents were withdrawing the case without prejudice. Therefore, this
matter is dismissed.

FINAL DECISION AND ORDER:

The matter is DISMISSED, without prejudice.
