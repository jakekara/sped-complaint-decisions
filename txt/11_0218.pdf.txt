FINAL DECISION AND ORDER

ISSUES:

1. Whether the Board failed to provide FAPE for 2009—2010, and 2010-2011 school
years;

2. Whether the Student required an out of district placement, and if so, whether that
placement should have been Eagle Hill for the 2010-2011 school year;

3. Whether the Board should have reimbursed the parents for the cost of the Eagle
Hill program and placement;

4. Whether the Board committed procedural and substantative violations.

SUMMARY:

On November 22, 2010, the Board received the Request for Due Process. On January 28, 201 1
the Parent withdrew the Request for Hearing and requested that the matter be dismissed.

FINAL DECISION AND ORDER:

The matter is DISMISSED.
