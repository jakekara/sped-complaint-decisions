Hearing Officer   FINAL DECISION AND ORDER  This hearing was requested by the Board.  The issues included the Board&#8482;s request to conduct a psychiatric evaluation and whether the student should be placed in a diagnostic placement at CES.  The prehearing in this matter was conducted on June 5, 2006.  Both 

parties participated and a hearing date that was agreed to was selected.  Subsequent to the 
prehearing conference the Parent retained counsel.  Prior to the commencement of the 

hearing the Board withdrew its request for due process, without prejudice.   FINAL DECISION AND ORDER:  The hearing is dismissed without prejudice.
