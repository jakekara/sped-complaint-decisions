FINAL DECISION AND ORDER

ISSUES:

1. Is the program offered by the Board for the 2013-2014 school year appropriate and does
it provide the Student with a free and appropriate public education (FAPE) in the least
restrictive environment (LRE)? If not;

2. Is the unilateral placement of the Student at Little Keswick School in Keswick, Virginia
appropriate and does it provide the Student with a meaningful education?

3. Should the Parents be reimbursed for cost incurred 0f the unilateral placement of the
Student at Little Keswick School in Keswick, Virginia for the 2013-2014 school year?

4. Should the Parents be reimbursed for the cost of the evaluation performed by Dr. Jeanne
Dietrich?

SUMMARY AND PROCEDURAL HISTORY:

The Student has been identiﬁed with Autism and is entitled to receive FAPE as deﬁned in the
Individuals With Disabilities Education Improvement Act (IDEA) 20 U.S.C. §1401 et seq. and
Connecticut General Statute §10—76a et seq. At a planning and placement team (PPT) meeting,
the Parents rejected the program offered by the Board for the 2013—2014 school year. The
Parents requested placement at Little Keswick School in Keswick, Virginia for the 2013—2014
school year. The Board refused the Parents’ request,

On February 18, 2014, the Board received notice of the Parents’ request for due process. The
patties agreed to forgo a resolution meeting and mediate the mattetx An impartial hearing ofﬁcer
was appointed on February 14, 2014 and a pre-hearing conference was held on February 18,
2014. A hearing date of March 27, 2014 was chosen by the parties.

In an electronic transmission the parties requested cancelation of the hearing date because the
matter had been resolved. The parties requested additional time in order to ﬁnalize an
agreement. In an electronic transmission dated April 9, 2014, the parties advised the hearing
ofﬁcer that the agreement had not been signed by all parties. The Parents’ attorney withdrew the
matter without prejudice.

The date for the mailing of the Final Decision and Order is May 2, 2014,

FINAL DECISION AND ORDER:

THE MATTER IS DISMISSED WITHOUT PREJUDICE.
