Mangs, Hearing Officer   FINAL DECISION AND ORDER   This hearing was requested on March 1, 2001. The parties participated in mediation on May 14, 2001 but were unable to resolve the case. The current hearing officer was appointed on May 16, 2001. A prehearing conference was convened on May 25, 2001. The hearing was convened on June 4, 2001 at which time the parties informed the hearing officer that they were close to an agreement. On June 14, 2001, the parents notified the hearing officer that an agreement had been prepared and was in the process of being executed by both parties. They requested that the hearing scheduled for June 14, 2001 be canceled. The hearing officer notified the parties that the hearing would be held open until June 18, 2001 at which time the case would be dismissed if it had not been withdrawn. As of June 25, 2001, the parties had not communicated again with the hearing officer.   FINAL DECISION AND ORDER:   This case is dismissed.
