FINAL DECISION AND ORDER
ISSUES:

1. Did the Board provide the Student FAPE during the 2010—2011 school year?
2, Is the Board providing the Student FAPE during the 201 1—2012 school year?

SUMMARY:

A special education hearing in the above—captioned matter was requested by the Student’s
attorney and received by the Board on February 15, 2012. On February 24, 2012, a pre-hearing
conference was held and hearing dates were scheduled. On April 3, 2012, the Parent withdrew
the Request for Due Process without prejudice.

FINAL DECISION AND ORDER:

The matter is DISMISSED without prejudice.
