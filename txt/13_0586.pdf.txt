FINAL DECISION AND ORDER

ISSUES:

1. Is the Board’s program for the Student for the 2013 Extended School Year appropriate?

2. Is the Board’s program for the Student for the 2013-2014 school year appropriate?

3. If the Board’s program for the Student for the 2013-2014 school year is not appropriate, is the
placement of Student at Ben Bi'onz Academy the appropriate placement?

PROCEDURAL HISTORY AND SUMMARY:

On June 28, 2013, the Windsor Board of Education received a Request for a Special Education Due
Process Hearing from Attorney for the Student. The undersigned hearing ofﬁcer was appointed on June
27, 2013. On July 17, 2013, a prehearing conference was held. During the preheating conference,
hearing dates were set and the deadline for the mailing of the Final Decision and Order was established
as September 11, 2013. The hearing was scheduled to take place on September 17, 2013, September 19,
2013, September 25, 2013, September 30, 2013 and October 2, 2013. Upon receipt ofthe Notice of
Hearing Dates, Attorney identiﬁed a scheduling conﬂict with the initial hearing date of September 17,
2013. Upon request by Attorney for the Parent and with no objection from Attorney for the Board, the
schedule of hearing dates was revised and September 19, 2013 was established as the date the hearing
would begin. An extension of the deadline for the mailing of the Final Decision and Order in order to
accommodate the hearing dates was requested by Attorney for the Student on August 23, 20131 This
request was rendered moot by the withdrawal of the hearing request in advance of the original mailing
date deadline. On August 29, 2013, Attorney for the Student withdrew the hearing request.

FINAL DECISION AND ORDER:

In light of the above facts, the case is dismissed.
