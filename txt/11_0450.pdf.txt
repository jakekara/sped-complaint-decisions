FINAL DECISION AND ORDER

ISSUES:

1. Whether the Board had knowledge that the Student was a child with a disability before
the behavior that resulted in the disciplinary action;

2. Whether the Student’s conduct which resulted in the pending disciplinary action was
caused by 01' had a direct and substantial relationship to the Student’s disability;

3. Whether the Student shall be identiﬁed as eligible for special education.

PROCEDURAL HISTORY/DISCUSSION:

This request for hearing was received by the Board on May 12, 2011 and the prehearing
conference convened on May 23, 201 1. After the prehearing conference, the Parent’s attorney
submitted notiﬁcation that the patties had reached a settlement agreement although the Parent
did not waive the Parent’s right to re-ﬁle for a due process hearing at a later date. Therefore, this
matter is dismissed, without prejudice.

FINAL DECISION AND ORDER:

The matter is DISMISSED, without prejudice.
