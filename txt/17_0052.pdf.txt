F INAL DECISION AND ORDER
ISSUES:

1. Did the District deny Student at Free Appropriate Public education during the period two
years preceding the ﬁling of the special education due process hearing request?

2. Should the District retain Student for an additional school year and provide special
education and related services during the 2016—2017 academic year?

PROCEDURAL HISTORY AND SUMMARY:

On J uly 28, 2016, the District received a request for a special due process hearing ﬁled by the
Parents. On July 28, 2016, the Connecticut State Department of Education appointed this
Hearing Ofﬁcer to preside over the case. On August 15, 2016, a preheating conference was held.
During the prehearing conference, hearing dates of October 4, 2016, October 6, 2016, October
17, 2016 and October 21, 2016 were set and the deadline for mailing the ﬁnal decision and order
was established as October 11, 2016. The parties subsequently settled the matter at the
resolution session and on August 30, 2016, Parents withdrew the request for a special education
hearing with prejudice.

FINAL DECISION AND ORDER:

In light of the above facts, the case is dismissed.
