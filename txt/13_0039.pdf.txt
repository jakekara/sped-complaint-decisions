FINAL DECISION AND ORDER

ISSUES:

1. Was the neuropsychological evaluation conducted by the Board appropriate?

2. If the Board’s neuropsychological evaluation was not appropriate, did the evaluation
obtained by the Parents meet agency criteria?

SUMMARY:

The hearing was requested by the Board on September 6, 2012. A preheating conference was
held on September 17, 2012 and a hearing date of November 14, 2012 was set. The original
deadline for the mailing of the ﬁnal decision and order was October 21, 2012. The parties
requested an extension of 30 days in order to engage in mediation. That request was granted and
November 20, 2012 was set as the new deadline for the mailing the ﬁnal decision and order. On
October 9, 2012, the Board withdrew its hearing request with prejudice.

FINAL DECISION AND ORDER:

In light of the above facts, the case is dismissed with prejudice.
