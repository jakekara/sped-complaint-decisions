  FINAL ORDER AND DECISION  ISSUES:  1. Is the program offered to the Student for the summer of 2004 appropriate? 
2. Is the program offered by the Board for the 2004-2005 school year 
appropriate? If not; 
3. Should the program for the 2004-2005 require: 
a. Adequate staffing training 
b. Integrated related therapies 
c. Opportunity for the Student to interact with peers 
d. Support staff for the Student 
e. Extended day program 
f. Direct involvement by regular ed teachers 
g. Involvement of a mutually accepted consultant
