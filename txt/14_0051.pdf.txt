PROCEDURAL HISTORY/SUMMARY:

The parents brought the Due Process Complaint and Request for Hearing on July 18,
2013. The Hearing Ofﬁcer was appointed the same day. The Hearing Ofﬁcer conducted
a prehearing conference on August 19, 2013 and a hearing was scheduled for September
18, 2013. On September 3, 2013, the parent’s attorney reported that the parties had
engaged in mediation with a state appointed mediator and remained in negotiations. On
September 13, 2013, the parent’s attorney requested a postponement of the hearing and
extension of the mailing dates to continue negotiations. The request was granted and the
hearing was rescheduled to October 15, 2013 and the mailing date of the Final Decision
was extended from October 1, 2013 to October 31, 2013. On October 3, 2013, the
parent’s attorney notiﬁed the Hearing Ofﬁcer that the parents and the board were
continuing to negotiate their dispute and requested that the Due Process Complaint and
Request for Hearing be withdrawn without prejudice.

FINAL DECISION AND ORDER:

The matter is DISMISSED without prejudice.
