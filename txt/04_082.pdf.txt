Hearing Officer   FINAL DECISION AND ORDER  ISSUE  Whether the child required an out of district placement to meet the requirements of a free 
and appropriate public education based on the child&#8482;s individual needs.   PROCEDURAL HISTORY  The parent requested a Due Process hearing on March 31, 2004.  At a prehearing conference on April 2, 2004 the parties agreed to a hearing date of May 5, 2004.  At a hearing on May 5, 2004 the parent was unable to attend the hearing and requested an additional hearing date be scheduled for May 17, 2004 if they were unable to reach an agreement before that date.  Neither party communicated the need for further hearing 
dates, the record closed on May 17, 2004.  FINAL DECISION AND ORDER  The case is dismissed with prejudice.
