FINAL DECISION AND ORDER
ISSUE:

Whether the Board’s transitional assessments are appropriate.

PROCEDURAL HISTORY/DISCUSSION:

The request for hearing was submitted by the Board on June 26, 2012 and a prehearing
conference convened on June 29, 2012.

On July 25, 2012 the Board submitted notiﬁcation that the Student’s request for an independent
educational evaluation [IEE] had been withdrawn. Based on the withdrawal of the request for an

IEE, the Board withdrew its request for a due process hearing. Therefore, this matter is
dismissed.

FINAL DECISION AND ORDER:

The matter is DISMISSED.
