FINAL DECISION AND ORDER

ISSUES:

1. Is the Student’s program appropriate?
2, If not, what are the appropriate remedies?

PROCEDURAL HISTORY:

The Student requested a special education hearing in the above—captioned matter by a
document dated February 7, 2013. This Impartial Hearing Ofﬁcer was appointed to the
case on February 13, 2013. A telephonic pre-hearing conference was conducted on
March 14, 2013. At that time, a second telephonic pre—hearing conference was scheduled

for April 5, 2013.

On March 28, 2013, the Student notiﬁed the Impaltial Hearing Ofﬁcer that the Student
and the Board of Education had resolved their dispute at mediation, and requested that

the matter be dismissed.

FINAL DECISION AND ORDER:

It is ordered that the Student’s request for dismissal is granted, and this case is dismissed.
