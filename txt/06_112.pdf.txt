Hearing Officer   FINAL DECISION AND ORDER  The issues in this matter concerned a manifestation determination made on April 11, 
2006 and a concurrent expulsion hearing.  The parent contended that she was having 
difficulty reaching anyone at the school due to the school&#8482;s vacation schedule. The 
request for due process was filed on April 24, 2006. A prehearing was scheduled for May 8, 2006 but prior to the prehearing being conducted the parent withdrew her request for due process.  FINAL DECISION AND ORDER:  The hearing is dismissed.
