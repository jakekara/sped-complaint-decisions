FINAL DECISION AND ORDER

ISSUES:

1. Whether the Board has failed to evaluate the Student in a 45 day diagnostic placement as

recommended by the PPT;

2. Whether the Student is entitled to compensatory education.

PROCEDURAL HISTORY/DISCUSSION:

The request for hearing was received by the Board on January 12, 2012 and a prehearing
conference convened on January 19, 2012. Counsel for the Board reported that a resolution

meeting was convened on February 15, 2012‘

The initial hearing date of March 6, 2012 was postponed at the request ofthe parties, and the
hearing was scheduled to convene on March 22, 2012. Prior to the ﬁrst hearing date, the
Parent’s attorney submitted notiﬁcation that the case was settled. Therefore, this matter is

dismissed.

FINAL DECISION AND ORDER:

The matter is DISMISSED.
