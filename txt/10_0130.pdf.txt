FINAL DECISION AND ORDER 
ISSUES: 1. Whether the Board failed to provide a copy of the Individual Educational Plan ("IEP")  to the Parent for a Planning and Placement Team meeting held in April, May, and June 2009; 
2. Whether the Board failed to comply with the Student's IEP for the 2008-2009 school year. 
SUMMARY: The Board received this request for hearing filed by the Parent on September 25, 2009.  A Pre-Hearing 
Conference was held on October 16, 2009.  During the 
Prehearing Conference, the Parent advised the Hearing 
Officer that she was withdrawing the Request for Due Process and a written request to withdraw the Request for 
Due Process was received thereafter.  
FINAL DECISION AND ORDER: 
The matter is DISMISSED, without prejudice.
