Appearing before: Attorney Elisabeth Borrino, Hearing Officer FINAL DECISION AND ORDER ISSUES: 1. Whether the Board failed to provide FAPE for the inclusive period of March 9, 2008 
through the end of summer 2008; 
2. Whether the Board failed to provide FAPE for the 2008-2009 school year, including 
extended year services; 3. Whether the Board failed to provide FAPE for the 2009-2010 school year; 
4. Whether the Board committed procedural violations; 
5. Whether the Board should provide one year of compensatory education to the Student. 
SUMMARY: On March 9, 2010, the Board received the Request for Due Process. On March 29, 2010, a 
Prehearing Conference was held and hearing dates were scheduled.  On April 23, 2010, Counsel for the Parent withdrew the Request for Due Process and requested that the matter be dismissed without 
prejudice. FINAL DECISION AND ORDER: The matter is 
DISMISSED without prejudice
