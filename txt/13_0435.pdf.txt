FINAL DECISION AND ORDER
ISSUES:

1. Did the Board fail to provide the Student with a Free Appropriate Public Education in the
Least Restrictive Environment for the 2011-2012 school year?

2. Did the Board fail to provide the Student with a Free Appropriate Public Education in the
Least Restrictive Environment for the 2012—2013 school year?

3. If the Board failed to provide the Student with a Free Appropriate Public Education for the
2011—2012 and/or 2012—2013 school years, is the Eagle Hill School the appropriate placement for

the Student?

PROCEDURAL HISTORY AND SUMMARY:

This special education hearing was requested on April 19, 2013. The undersigned hearing
ofﬁcer was appointed on April 22, 2013. On May 3, 2013, a prehearing conference was held and
hearing dates were set for June 17, 2013 and June 24, 2013. The original deadline for the mailing
of the ﬁnal decision and order was established as July 3, 2013. On June 17, 2013, the patties
appeared for the hearing and requested an extension of the deadline of the ﬁnal decision and
order in order to engage in mediation. This request was granted and the revised deadline for the
mailing of the ﬁnal decision and order was set as August 2, 2013. Additional hearing dates of
July 23, 2013, July 24, 2013 and July 25, 2013 were selected. On July 23, 2013, the third
scheduled date of hearing, the parties reached a settlement and Counsel for the Parents withdrew
the hearing request on the record.

FINAL DECISION AND ORDER:

In light of the above facts, the case is dismissed.
