 FINAL DECISION AND ORDER 
  ISSUES:    1. Whether the Board improperly expelled the Student. 
2. Whether the Board failed to evaluate
 the Student in a timely manner. 
3. Whether the Board failed to offer the Student the assistance 
of an appropriate tutor. 4. Whether the Board failed to implement the 
Student&#8482;s IEP to transfer the Student 
to a specialized private setting. 5. Whether the Student is entitled to compensatory education.  SUMMARY:  The Board received this request for hearing on May 14.  On or about June 15, 2007, the parties resolved this matter.  The 
hearing officer was not notified of this resolution in a timely manner before the hear
ing convened on June 27.  On June 26,  the Board representative confirmed that the matter had been settled.   
 FINAL DECISION AND ORDER:
 The matter is 
DISMISSED.
