FINAL DECISION AND ORDER

ISSUE:

Whether the Student’s program for the 2012-13 school year is inappropriate in that his job
placements do not sufﬁciently target his communication weaknesses and his interests.
PROCEDURAL HISTORY/DISCUSSION:

The request for hearing was received by the Board on September 6, 2012 and a preheating
conference convened on September 13, 2012.

On October 15, 2012, prior to the ﬁrst hearing date, the Parent submitted a request that the case
be dismissed without prejudice. The Parent indicated that she plans to reﬁle a hearing request at
a later date, but wanted time to confer with her attorney who is currently Out of the country.
Therefore, this matter is dismissed.

FINAL DECISION AND ORDER:

The matter is DISMISSED, without prejudice.
